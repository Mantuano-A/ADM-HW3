After watching the previous Kamisama Hajimemashita seasons/episodes I was really looking forward to watch this OVA. I was sad and excited for the ending of one of my favourite series so far.

However, after completing this OVA I can say that my expectations were too high. To begin with, the OVA was really short (12 minutes), it seemed like a lot of scenes were missing. As one of the other reviewers said it felt like it was rushed, which I kinda agree to. This short ending left a lot of questions for me unanswered. I did not understand why certain things happened, etc. Furthermore I feel like the progression of characters wasn't shown in this OVA, specially Tomoe's. To sum up I'd say this OVA was slightly disappointing and confusing.

On the other hand, the romance and the warmth of the anime stayed along with the music and visuals. It was really nice to experience it once again.

Overall, I would give this OVA a 7/10:
