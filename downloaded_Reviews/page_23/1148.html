Well, this was a good little OVA. This review isn't gonna be all that long considering it's only one episode, but I'll be happy to tell you all what I think anyway.

This is an OVA that focuses on Kunikida, which I think is very much needed as a part of the narrative of Bungou Stray Dogs. The main storyline doesn't focus much at all on his character, so getting this dedicated time for him is really nice. Again, since this is a singular episode, the story feels a bit out of nowhere, but that's to be expected. What it did do, though, was provide a window into Kunikida's character that we haven't seen before. We know of his ideals and tenacity, but through this OVA we really get to see what makes him such a strong character.

It's a shame that we don't get to see it more in the main story, but this OVA does offer a bit of development regardless, so at least the viewer can get a bit of context while watching down the road. If you like his character, I think you'll like this OVA. If nothing else, it's pretty badass to watch. So, yeah. I liked it a lot.
