Pre-review TLDR:
Two words: Murakami Teruaki. Those should be good enough for any hentai enthusiast to get a mind's image of what to expect. And that is nothing but some of the best, most depraved hentai action out there. Ahegao, torture, bondage, mind break, dudes taking turns all day. Pretty standard stuff to expect from the glorious Murakami. If you know of Teruaki's works and are not a fan of them then this one won't be any different from his other works so sorry guys. But if you're a big fan, like me, or have yet to see his works and is interested in the things I listed a few sentences ago, then enjoy yourselves my dudes.


The Good:

• The art. As usual, character designs are pretty great. They don't have much to offer for those wishing for smaller racks, but for everyone wishing for large ones, rejoice.

• The plot. Centered around a dude who claims himself to be the legendary pirate who was apparently killed 300 years ago and his quest for vengeance. It's not really anything too special, but unique enough to get those interested enough in the plot to actually await future developments and possible revelations.

• The scenes and animation. Yep it's Murakami directed alright. Fast paced animation, plentiful scenes with little breaks in between to leave no time for you to catch your breath. Anyone familiar with Teruaki will still see his trademark animation direction style that we all know and love(mostly).

The bad:

• The art. With most of the art and animation focus on the H scenes, oftentimes during plot segments, art and animation quality will seem to take a dip such as being faceless, awkward or unmoving mouths and faces and the likes. But surely a small gripe and sacrifice for quality H scene animation and content.

Things that may turn people off:

Scat
Torture
Bondage
Hardcore action
Rape
Pissing

Final Verdict:
Two Teruaki directed shows in one year, truly something every fan would dream of. Another solid release from him and from this years lineup and would most likely be enjoyed by any hardcore hentai fan, whether they are also a fan of Teruaki or not. Eagerly awaiting episode2 now.

This hentai follows the formula seen in many others, which began, I think, with the far-famed fantasy hentai Kuroinu: Kedakaki Seijo wa Hakudaku ni Somaru: beautiful and hot women who previously held powerful posts, like being members of the royalty, are defeated and captured by perverted men just to be mercilessly raped and become sex toys. In fact, I found this hentai quite similar to Kuroinu, despite being shorter.

All scenes are sexual (apart from short intervals), and all sex is violent rape of the said hot royal chicks. The scenes are vivid, with lots of semen, groans, spanking and gangbangs, so if you are hungry for rough pornography, this hentai is just for you. Just like in Kuroinu, all women seen are raped, even those you may think they won't get it.

If you care for the setting and storyline, it goes more or less as follows (spoilers ahead): it is set in the country of Austria apparently two or three centuries ago (of course, a totally fantastic version of the said period). The rulling princesses fail to protect the country from a pirate invasion, so the pirates take over. But one of the princesses is also betrayed by one of her fellow royal men, who sides with the pirates and... the rest you already guessed. It all actually begins in media res when the princesses are about to be publicly raped by hundreds of naked citizens for being a shame to the country. Most lines are things said during sex, so the story is really secondary here.

So here's my conclusion: if rape and fantasy settings of this kind aren't your thing, you might want to search for something else. Otherwise, go ahead, this is a great one.
