Some hentai which the story of a teacher who is making love, Kaito Shiomi is a teacher from Minatohashi Higashi Academy, When her mother passes away suddenly, Shinozaki Sumire finds herself alone and without a means to support herself. Out of desperation she turns to the message board Free Friends to earn some money. She develops a relationship with her first partner, Kaito, without realizing that they’re much more than strangers ...

A few days later, he discovers that “Sumi” is not only a student at the exact same school he teaches at, but that her classmates call her “Sumire”. Incredulous, Kaito checks the school rosters. Surely it’s not the same Sumire he knows; surely it’s just another girl with the same given name? But as if setting him up for the universe’s biggest punchline, the only Sumire at the school is none other than his very own Shinozaki Sumire, the sister his mother had taken away from him. Kaito’s dreams of one day reuniting with his sister have in fact already been realized… just not in a way that he ever could have imagined.

The animation in the show is generally very fluid, and although there are some notable flaws, they don't actually impede on the enjoyment of the show. The color palette used is very good and suitable, some colors look very contrasting even though this is a Hentai anime but they are not shy about art quality.

The background music and erotic sounds are also very good, in addition to animation the sound part is also very important from the anime with the Hentai theme. This is also a good series, in terms of the story when paired with sound in my opinion this is the best. I mean if you see the essence of the Hentai series out there, most of them are only concerned with the sound and character parts, while the parts of their stories are actually very bad and that's what makes me hate some Hentai series.

Furifure 2 is the best Hentai anime series this year, in my opinion. The adaptation of "Visual Novel" I hope this will be better than the version of "Visual Novel" but unfortunately it only gets four episodes of anime adaptation, even though they can make it more.
