Aniyome we Ijippari, also known as Lover-in-Law, has a very simple premise. Tsutomu lives with his brother's voluptuous wife, who he has the hots for. She is quite lonely and unsatisfied, since her husband is rarely home due to his work; at the same time a friend of Mai, Kozue, is hired to work at the Flower Shop that Mai runs. Kozue is also very well-endowed, and likewise feels a little lonely.

Yes, Tsutomu is a very lucky guy.

Eventually Tsutomu and Mai get intimate, and later on Kozue gets a piece of the action. Personality-wise, characters are nice, gentle and kind to each other. There are no villains here trying to kidnap, blackmail or enslave anyone. The male lead is ordinary looking, and the females are quite the eye candy. Keep an eye out for the Bikini scene in Episode 2!

The color palette is quite varied, no excesive grayness that could depress viewers. The Japanese dub is well-done, the background music and the sound effects are adequate for each situation.

This is a great title for newer Hentai watchers to start with. All of the sex is fully consensual and quite romantic, and there are no bizarre acts involving, say, body waste or anything similar... although when Tsutomu gets his hands on a remote controlled vibrator, things become a little kinkier, yet he still treats Mai and Kozue with respect through both episodes.

If you really are in the mood for a good, romantic Hentai, that doesn't involve endless torment and humilliation for well-endowed female characters, then this will be your cup of tea.

In request of a certain friend, I wrote this review.

Lover in Law, just like the title suggests, is a Hentai that involves love which surpass the boundary of moral in society. This Hentai is about Tsutomu who is living with his older brother and his wife, Mai, while helping Mai runs her flower shop from time to time. As his brother is absorbed with his work and is too busy to go home, it creates a rift within Mai’s heart. This is nothing new as we, humans, seek the presence of somebody because let’s be honest here, nobody wants to live alone in this world. Not just that, unlike what most people says, this anime goes into quite deep into the problem that a typical couple nowadays face. This review will be divided into two parts, looking this Hentai as a “material” and as a piece of art, an anime.

Hentai

Unlike many other Hentai nowadays that typically degrades women and focus on mind break/rape, this Hentai is a must watch for those who wants to see a Hentai where the genders are treated as equals. Not just that, this Hentai is a Jack of All Trades because of how many genres are being compiled into one such as MILF, Incest, Yuri, and many more. Furthermore, the Hentai sets up its’ “material” pretty nicely as each sexual intercourse gradually becomes better and more intense which is great for those who takes time during their “research”. From the start, we are given a slideshow of characters that acts as the introduction which is helpful to us to see the characters that we’ll watch and who we should focus on.

For a Hentai that has almost pass a decade, the animation is great and there is a great OST to back the Hentai up from time to time. The animation and character design is nothing unique, but it does its jobs as a “material”, although it pays quite a lot of details into its background and environment which is great. The OST fit right into the Hentai and it does its jobs of setting up the mood for the viewers, which is quite important for the viewers to perform their “research”. Not just that, haven’t I mention that this Hentai is UNCENSORED which is quite rare nowadays, hence it’s a great watch for those who are sick of the ridiculous Japanese censorship. Lastly, the Seiyuus (Voice Actors/Actresses) for this Hentai are spot on and their performance during certain scenes allow us to feel immersed when watching this Hentai while doing our “research”.

For a Hentai, it’s a great watch for those who wish to watch Vanilla oriented Hentai.

Anime

No matter the age and gender, all humans seek pleasure. It’s something that we as human beings are programmed to have and it’s not something that we could switch on and off easily. This could be seen from how our characters have sexual intercourse with each other despite their age barrier and morality. From the outside, it seems that this is a relationship that must not occur, but in the past, society saw same-sex gender relationship as a taboo, then what’s stopping these characters from falling in love with each other? Love is blind and it knows no boundary, we can’t choose who we love as it happens naturally.

At first glance, there seems to be nothing special about the story, but if we open our mind and try to look at this anime at another angle, we could see that there’s something more to be served. In general, the story is about how the main character, Tsutomu, ends up having sex with his stepsister, Mai, and her friend, Kozue. However, this anime is a depiction of what seems to be a common problem in a typical Japanese family where the husband/head of the family would work extremely hard for the family to support the family in exchange of their time together. This Hentai shows the dilemma that many husband must face nowadays in order to reach the path of happiness, either they must spend less time with the family, though earn more for them or vice versa. By sacrificing a person’s time with his/her family, he/she must face the consequence that their partner may seek pleasure and someone’s presence. The story of this Hentai is not a fiction as such story also happens in real-life and it’s not something to be taken lightly. When we lose the sight of what once was our goal, we often wish that we could go back in time and choose differently, but life is not a fantasy. This is what the ending music at the end of the Hentai represents, humans tend to oversee the worth of someone/something until we lose them.

Overall, this Hentai is worth a watch if you’re looking for Hentai that’s relaxing and can help you with your “research”.

/// Story ///
The story lacks a clear protagonist (which is not necessarily a bad thing in this case), and is seen from a mixed perspective (i.e., the perspectives of both male and female characters), and while definitely not bad, the story is nothing out of the ordinary; it is one of those run-of-the-mill hentai stories where a woman who is lonely and in need of romantic and/or sexual gratification ends up in a sexual relationship with one of her in-laws (in this case, her stepson). The story is thus, rather unoriginal, but this is in large made up for with good character development. Furthermore, in my opinion, the ending of the second episode felt a bit rushed, and I consider this to be the by far most damning aspect of the anime as a whole.


/// Character Development ///
The personality of the characters, along with their backstories, are developed and relatable enough for one to feel invested (at least on a superficial level), and the characters come across as far more natural and less superficial than your average anime character, which is in large part thanks to the highly (but not overly) expressive dialogue and facial expressions of the characters.


/// Art-style and Visual Oddities ///
The anime was released in 2008 and retains most of that 1990~2007 art-style that many of us remember with fondness and feelings of nostalgia. However, the animation is very sloppy at times and certain body-parts are way out of proportion; most notably, the neck of Katsuragi Tsutomu, whose neck looks about as stretched out as that of Kayan women (link for article/pictures: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kayan_people_(Myanmar) ). The thing in particular that bothers me a lot about Tsutomu's long neck is that all of the other characters have necks of relatively average length. Another visual oddity that stands out is the uniformity in the coloration of some body-parts (e.g., the shaft of the penis and the penis glans having the exact same color), but this is rather common in older anime and can to some extent be excused or justified given the times.


/// Verdict ///
Overall, a solid old-school hentai that is undeniably a product of its time; it is not genteel, but it has that sense of innocence that is so typical of many older hentai, but which is largely absent in more modern hentai (which are generally more sinister and hardcore, both in terms of mood and subject-matter). If you like sex scenes which are more intimate and less about fucking each-others' brains out, and if you, like me, like old school animation, you should definitely give this hentai a try.
