Taboo Charming Mother, referred to as TCM in this review, is beyond a regular hentai. It's as if the director of this glorious masterpiece had a vision from the heavens telling him to create this piece of art.

Story:
The story is probably the strongest aspect of the show. TCM is full of surprises, unexpected turns, and great storytelling that keep the viewer on the edge of his seat. Not once has TCM failed to arouse me or keep me guessing.

Art:
Every person I showed TCM to was amazed by how advanced and beautiful the animation was. They compared TCM to a early 2000's version of Fate/stay night unlimited bladeworks, in my opinion, however; TCM is miles ahead of Fate in terms of animation. The animation never fails to capture the full lewdness of the anime.

Sound:
Extremely realistic... that summarizes the sound the best. It's almost as if the voice actors actually engaged in the activities in the show. The amount of effort and time put in to this anime's voice acting is astounding. I can only imagine how hard it was to create such a realistic and believable environment for the viewer, to the point where the viewer can imagine himself, or herself... I don't judge, as the main character of the story.

Character:
TCM has very well-rounded, authentic, and unique characters that tie the show in a neat bow. All of the characters, whether main or background, are fully "explored" to the point where the viewer knows every little detail about each character. The creator of this stellar work of art is an absolute genius and master of characterization and story telling.

Enjoyment:
I can say without a doubt in this world that I enjoyed this paragon of a hentai with every fiber of my being - literally.

Conclusion:
TCM is by far the greatest piece of television that has ever been or will be created. The vivid story telling, authentic animation, and realistic voice acting all make up the embodiment of a perfect anime.
