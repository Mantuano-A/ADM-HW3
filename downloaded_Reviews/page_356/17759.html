Hell yes! When combing through the mound of hentai on the internet like you comb your pompadour, it's difficult to find something that makes you think (and sometimes even say!) "Wow! This is awesome!" This is one of those. It's pretty awesome. The number of times I find it hard to review something because of it's terrible average-ness can be grating, but this is super awesome. I am a girl, like I say in most of my hentai reviews, and so my taste may be different to guys who watch this stuff. So, sorry in advance if I mislead you or whatever, but each to their own, right?

Story is original, and is awesome. We have the transfer student, Charlotte, who is a pretty funky character. She has one of the most detailed character designs I've seen in a hentai, and her abilities and personality make her a memorable hentai character. Quite a rare specimen, I know. She is genuinely gorgeous and a pleasure to look at. Anyway, she has a supernatural thing going on with her. She can switch between being invisible and being visible, she also has a supernatural pair of glasses which she gives to our protagonist guy, Maejima. The glasses are a lovely perk, and are original. I find it very interesting when there are hentai that use objects like these to add some spice to a series. The glasses let him see whatever he wants to see, so obviously he sees all of his female classmates naked when he puts them on. It's a lovely twist that I enjoyed immensely. The first sexual scene that happens is about 3 minutes in, and involves Charlotte giving Maejima oral sex under the desk. It's one of my favourite hentai scenes, and is so goddamn memorable that it would be dangerous if I remembered/fantasized about it too often in class. His reactions, her mind reading, it all comes together (no pun intended) to make a pretty awesome scene. Oh yeah, I forgot to mention that she can read minds. It is used amaaazingly in this scene, where we hear the internal monologue of Maejima as he is getting head under his desk. He thinks about what he wants her to do to him, and she does it. It's so originally done and awesome. I loved it! On top of that, you have the factor of the C-thru glasses, so he is looking at all of his classmates naked and he is a pretty awesome dude.

The animation isn't great and looks awkward, clunky and cheap in some scenes. But I prefer that to just seeing one well-animated position in the sexy times. I would prefer to get interesting scenes at the cost of animation quality. It's not so bad that it's distracting, it's just a bit crap during some of the... fast paced... bits. The character designs, however, are lovely. Definitely above average. Charlotte is really pretty and unique, her red eyes are quite striking and make her memorable. They also let you know that she is no ordinary character! I talk about her a bit more under "Characters" (see what I did there? It was another joke...). Maejima's character design is worth mentioning, too. He's just a cool guy. He looks like the main guy from Mushi-Shi but with black hair. The bit of hair covering one of his eyes is awesome! He's definitely not an eyesore, although he is plainer than the girls to appeal to male audiences. I found him to be quite attractive, actually.

Sound. The voice acting is well above standard, with sex noises that are pretty amazing and music that fits the scenes well. The music isn't very noticable since it's a hentai, but it is still fine to have in the background. The sex noises of both Charlotte and Maejima are brilliant, and Charlotte manages to pull off the moe female character without making her feel underage at all. An admirable feat!

Characters! Let's start with the worst: Shiraishi, the class rep. She's as bland as they come (no pun intended), with her only trait being that she has a crush on Maejima. At least, I think she does... Anyway! She contributes a lengthy masturbation scene and sex scene too, one right after the other. I'm sure you can guess how that comes about. She's your classic "good girl gone bad" character in a hentai. Now, let's move on to the awesome characters. Charlotte is actually a ________, so she has supernatural abilities! It's pretty awesome how they factor into the sex scenes, and keeps this interesting and fresh. She has a very cute character design, with long golden hair and red bows in it. Her red eyes are pretty original in anime, and I don't recall seeing them on a girl except for in Myself;Yourself. She's a cute character with an interesting background and cute sidekick bunny wabbit. Our protagonist guy, Maejima, is as relatable as is possible in a hentai. He's not the kind of character that is disgustingly pervy, so he's likeable. He's just like all of us: sweet most of the time but a bit pervy when he gets turned on. It's not the nasty kind of pervy, either. It's the hormone-induced, schoolboy kind of pervy. And I liked it!

Enjoyment! I loved watching every second of this. It has enough sex to be fappable, but not so much story that it gets bogged down by it (I'm looking at you, Daiakuji the Xena Buster!). The sex scene between Charlotte and Maejima is hot, and the whole "sex at school" thing is really awesome. I didn't really like the aphrodisiac-induced sex with Shiraishi because she acts very slutty and I don't like slutty girls in hentai at all. I did enjoy it when Maejima was slipped an aphrodisiac by Charlotte so they could have sex for longer. That was cool. The amount of orgasms this guy had was crazy, though! It did make me laugh a points, because about 2 thrusts later, he'd say, "I'm coming again! I just can't stop coming!" It was pretty funny, intentional or not.

Overall, it's awesome. Just so much goodness, seriously. Watch this. Please. You are betraying your loins if you don't. It's amazing, original, and very hot too. The story isn't bad and is quite intriguing, actually. Now, go and watch it!

The result is a notable short erotic anime. The animation is somewhat basic, but serves its purpose, supplemented by superior voice acting and sound effects as the protagonist and his erotic conquests imaginatively disport themselves in various academic settings. Highly enjoyable and recommended to anyone with a bed corner to grind against.
