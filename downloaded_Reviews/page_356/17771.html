Futabu (or "Futa Club"), an adaptation of a manga by Bosshi, delivers exactly what it's name promises: An entire club of girls with dicks (as well as a "token normal girl"). Naturally, the sex heavily involves their extra appendages, so this hentai is purely for futanari lovers. And those are catered to well, with a diverse cast of pretty futas with varying body, breast and, of course, dick sizes (with the loli having the biggest one). Naturally, characterization is light, but all characters are rather endearing.

As a typical story-less sex romp, it's mostly a sequence of sex scenes, in which the futas do it with the "normal" girl and each other. These leave nothing to be desired (unless you want something that doesn't involve girl dicks) and are realized very well, with good (but not amazing( animation. Bosshi's character designs are also converted well (although they don't look quite as good as in the manga) and all the girls, and their "features" such as their breasts, are nice to look at.

Sadly, with all those dicks (especially a particularly huge one) flailing around, the obligatory censorship is more visible and therefore more annoying. Especially since the genitals appear to be drawn well too (as well as one can make out under the pixelation).

The sound is pretty good, with the wet sounds during the sex and the moans of the girls adding quite something erotic to it. Music is just there and not very notable.

Overall, it's a great vanilla-ish hentai for futanari fans. People who don't share that taste won't find much here, though.

Futa-bu, a club of futanari high school girls fucking each other, you will hate yourself for watching this show but at the same time you will be glad you did. Out of the many hentai I have watched this series takes the cake as being one of the most rewatchable with scenes that did not take up a whole episode's runtime and was engaging to the audience.

Story: 8

The show does not have much of a plot and it is more of a Slice of Life style show with several plots going on at the same time per episode similarly to how yonkoma anime adaptations are structured. The mini-plots themselves are amusing and the comedic beats hit more times than not. Together with how the character's are portrayed as over-exaggerated tropes, undercutting the situation with humor works perfectly.

Art: 8

The artstyle is given an average score of 7 as it is enjoyable enough and the softer style compliments the lighthearted tone of the show but really there is not much to say about it. Basically it is not something the viewer will take too much note of but it is there and doesn't hinder the show in anyway.

Sound: 7

Similarly to the artstyle, the sound is average, the voice actors did an excellent job and the performances never went so over the top as to take the viewer out of the experience. One point to note is that the show has a pleasant opening song that introduces the characters in character slides at the start of the episode and for a hentai having an opening song that is enjoyable to boot gives it an above average standing overall

Characters: 8

Where I would love to give the show credit most is with the characters. The overplayed tropes that the characters embody compliments the comedic tones of the show very well and helps the comedy flow well throughout an episode. Although mainly the characters are basically caricatures of certain tropes it must also be said that we usually see the character's personality develop throughout an episode, usually through intercourse, where we see a new level of depth to the character. As mentioned at the start I would love to give this section a higher grade but the introduction character slides break the 'show, don't tell' rule in storytelling and I feel that the show suffers from this misstep and could have avoided it all together and the character quirks would still have come across loud and clear.

Overall the uniqueness of the show's concept as well as the execution make for an excellent show to watch and although some may find futanari grotesque, for fans or those who do not mind the weird twists in hentai will definitely enjoy this show for it's entertainment value and mostly be engaged throughout the show. The show starts strong and finishes strong in the first season and the further character development in the second season really strengthens the show's foundation
