As the extremely generic moe-ecchi-ish name that "Tsun Tsun Maid wa Ero Ero Desu" (it sounds like some weird magical girl chant) suggests, this is a hentai catering to people with a maid fetish (with a few tsundere elements too). This short two episode series basically consists of about five sex scenes while our main couple slowly grows closer to each other through several comedic and slightly romantic situations.

The beginning of the story is kind of confusing and hardly gives any details for why our protagonist's (Eishun) childhood friend (Kiriha) is now his erotically dressed maid. Being a tsundere, Kiriha seems to have some feelings for Eishun, but she goes about showing it indirectly by playing "games" with him and acting like a somewhat normal maid (at first). Eishun secretly takes pictures of Kiriha in her outfit, but he is caught by her when he starts to masturbate to them. From there, it's basically just all sex in a few different scenarios with Kiriha slowly coming to enjoy it more and more.

The art is a little hit-or-miss. Kiriha's design and some of the animation of her during the sex scenes (and non-sex scenes) is very good. But that's only some of the time. Other times, the scenes just seem like repeating gifs that sometimes go for almost 15-20 seconds at a time and are occasionally reused. The series also has a problem of showing other things during the sex scenes like Kiriha's hedgehog sleeping or just the still room around them. The quality especially drops during the second episode.

As for the sound.....I don't ever remember hearing any music at all. It may have been there, but it was so unnoticeable behind everything else that I completely missed it. Still, Kiriha has some awesome moaning which gets better and better as she gets more into the sex later on. I liked her VA a lot in general and that was one of my favorite parts of the show along with her design.

I enjoyed the first episode more than the second one, but I would still recommend watching it all. Both episodes are only 15 minutes long, and they fulfill their "purpose." Especially if you like maids or tsundere girls in hentai you should check this out. The animation may falter at times, but Kiriha is still a very good female hentai protagonist and her moaning is great to listen to. There's also some enjoyable comedy mixed in between the sex scenes, and the series overall is very lighthearted for a hentai.

For a premise that seems generic as all hell, Tsun Tsun Maid wa Ero Ero Desu somehow manages to make the most out of it. While it shamelessly caters to the specific demographic of maid fetishes and tsunderes/childhood friends, the concrete direction allows the hentai to round the characters fairly well. We all like our "round" characters in hentai. ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)

Essentially the whole entire story is founded on two friends playing a game. The MC male (Eishun) is a horndog for maids, so that's where the whole maid theme comes from. Inevitably, this game ends up being MC female's (Kiriha) excuse to have sex with the guy.

The story isn't anything to write home about. The art isn't even stellar when compared to other hentais out there. And the sounds are generic, though the same can be said with 90% of the hentais out there so that doesn't mean much. Where this hentai excels is in its execution of the setup.

The combination of Kiriha's "maid" justification and tsundere personality, it ends up being close to himedere--princess like. It's due to her haughty teasing demeanor that seamlessly transitions to Eishun's perverse reaction against her. That's when Kiriha takes the bold direction to have sex with the one she loves, and Eishun only sees it as teasing gone too far. However, when Kiriha's boldness breaks due to hymen breaking, there is a paradigm shift. Eishun takes the lead as he realizes it wasn't just a tease. Finally, it ends with a consensual sex theme.

This dynamic sense of progression flows with how dynamic the sex is. On top of this, due to the general theme it seems almost like a himedere hentai due to the lines spoken. However, the general attitude is that of a tsundere which ends up meaning there are two themes simultaneously in the same events.

The characters have stellar development for a 2 episode hentai. The events at the very beginning of the first episode efficiently and seamlessly builds their character with a simple dream sequence and a single conversation. Then the sex rounds out of their personalities. This makes the characters quite likeable and even relatable.

While Tsun Tsun Maid wa Ero Ero Desu is low tier in its story, art, and sound, I rewatched this multiple times simply because I adore the characters. To look past quality art in a hentai for the characters and choose it over other high quality hentais is remarkable.
