"Bungou Stray Dogs Wan!" definitely ended up being one of my favorite shows of this season (as it's been going so far). It's a silly show with short, fun episodes that are only about 11 minutes each. If you're already a huge fan of BSD, then this is definitely the show for you, but even if you aren't super into it, I think "Wan!" could be a good place to get more into the characters and their shenanigans.

Of course, it's important to note that "Wan!" has such a different tone from the original series. It has a few serious moments here and there, but is mostly joking. If you go into the main series expecting the same thing... You will be very disappointed lol. (The main series is absolutely incredible, though! Just much more seinen).

Some shorts were better than others, I'll admit. There were some episodes where a short would just cut off in the middle of what I thought was the build-up. There's a possibility that this was for time's sake or even maybe because of translation (Japanese humor has shocked me before), but either way I wished they were a little longer. In the end, though, each episode has to fit in about 3 shorts, and they still manage to be super funny and sweet! My favorites are the shorts in Episode 7!

Overall, I'm really gonna miss "Wan!" and all the fun interactions and adventures it gave us. And the art style! Oh, how I'll miss the cute chibi art style!

A cute chibi animation based of the spin-off gag manga by Kanaineko.

I am a huge fan of Bungo Stray Dogs so this review maybe quite biased;) But I believe in order to really enjoy BSD Wan! is to have watched all seasons of the original main series.

There are 12 episodes in total each being around 11 minutes each. Firstly the character designs are so adorable. We get to see people that normally don’t meet each other have interactions. We also get a better understanding of each individual’s personality’s, this makes the certain characters who normally don’t have much screen time stand out. The emotions, and chaotic energy each character prevails is delivered so well by the Japanese voice actors.

There are many wholesome and memorable moments, as well a special episodes such as Chuuya and Dazai as 14 year olds, a preschool segment and the Dark era which are all very nostalgic. I would say Episodes 1-9 were very funny, and would like to give a little warning that the last 3 episodes are quite emotional, however it does wrap up nicely in the end.

The sound track is also very comedic, and the ending outro is a nice little nod back to the original S1 ending track but sang by Atsushi’s voice actor in a more cheerful way. The outro animation also changes from time to time depending on what happens in that episode so please don’t skip it!

As a fan, I looked forward to each week for the new episode to air, and honestly it provided great humour and joy. Its sweet and short so I’ll probably rewatch it here and then. I was never quite ready for a S4 of BSD so this was a perfect way to provide content for those who have been longtime fans of the series. I hope we will get to see more of Wan! in the future:)

You like Bungou Stray Dogs? You want to watch a light hearted, comedic anime? Then Bungou Stray Dogs Wan! will be perfect for you!

It's a comedic anime, so it doesn't have an outstanding plot or unforeseen twists, but it doesn't matter. It focuses on comedy and the comedy is really great. Though it went kinda dark (for a comedy anime) in the last two or three episodes, it's definitely an anime that will cheer you up if you feel sad (especially at this time).
The characters behave just like they normally would and you can also see characters interacting with each other who normally don't even meet.

I'd recommend watching this if you are a fan of Bungou Stray Dogs. If you don't know the original anime though I would recommend you to watch the three seasons that are out until now, because it's just more enjoyable if you know the characters and their relationships.

I personally enjoyed this show very much, hence the 10/10.

BGS Wan! Is Bungou Stray Dogs' try on a Garupa*Pico etc. Kind of short series of gags and jokes. This series consists of 12*8 minute episodes.

The story is all about extra gags and funny situations the characters end up with. The series gets an overdose of comedy and drama which helps it dish out a hilarious show that resulted in a really fun watching experience. 7/10 though because these stories are not exactly original

The art is pretty comedic and charming, the characters look small and their heads are larger than normal. The animation is really smooth, the characters are saturated and vivid without much detail while the backgrounds also don't have too much detail and are less powerful and colourful so the characters stand out. 9/10 because I found it fun

The voice acting the pretty good but the real star of the sound category was the ending, such a beautiful and charismatic song. The song gets a 9.5/10 and the sound an 8 for not having an op

The characters are really expressive and unique. Especially in these comedy-centered series the characters only show some of their views and personality points in order to make jokes about them and their habits, I found these pretty good and the characters are still pretty complex and interesting

Overall this anime was super funny and entertaining, the story and characters remain simple, the ed is amazing and the art style is pretty charming and cute. In my opinion if you're gonna watch BGS you should totally watch this anime. It's just 2 hours of guaranteed good time and most of the jokes made me smile or laugh, it's just cute and funny which I liked. 8/10
