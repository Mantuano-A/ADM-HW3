The synopsis is all about character development!At the beginning, Gorou is in his regular colour scheme.After a bit, he changes into a hue of purple.The color purple is associated with ambition.He had an ambition to change.Spiritually,Mentally and Physically.Then he changes into a hue of the colour green.Green carries the vibration of growth, new beginnings, health, renewal, harmony, hope, and peace.He was going through a 177013(Metamorphosis) and was quickly changing.He felt at peace when he sensed the change.He then changed into different colors, which meant he had accepted everything that is his(His persona and true identity).At the beginning,the dance pattern was looping but in the end, there were more moves added (ending with his hand in the air).I could see that he was truly happy.
