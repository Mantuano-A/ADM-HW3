If you have been in the hentai community for long enough (atleast on MAL), you will know that the golden age of modern golden age of hentai ended in the late 2015s. Sure they're have still been some great hentai's but as far as the 2016 and 2017 years go, it is pretty disappointing. But 2019 has really been proving to be a year of reckoning and the greatest example of this is the hentai that is managing to break past the threshold of the three hentai that stop atop the hentai community for so many years and is well on it's way to steal the crown for first. I am of course talking about: "Master Piece The Animation".

Story: 7/10.
The story in Master Piece while being a simply harem story on the surface actually manages to accomplish a lot on the inside through it's characters. It has its dumb shit sure, but as far as pacing and actual writing of story goes it is good, but lacking.

Art and Animation: 9/10.
While I do strongly believe that Pink Pineapple is pretty overrated, it can't be denied that when they need to they can produce some damn good looking hentai's. I think my only real grudge is the same issue I had with 'Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai', the art can look a bit too superficial and lose it's realism, which I get is the point... but as far as animation goes... its Pink Pineapple bois, nothing else needs to be said. As for the choreography it too is fucking amazing to witness.

Sound: 10/10.
They hold no bars for this hentai and get some amazing VAs for all the characters and the bg music is great too.

Character: 8/10.
So the cast of harem basically consists of a family of two sisters and a mom... but guess what? The family actually looks like they are related to each other... big shocker. While I can appreciate that from a realism point of view from a reviewer mind set it's kinda upsetting. The whole point of harem is to have a cast of characters that are different in design and personality, thus appealing to a mass audience. The problem is not personalty's, all three are brimming with that, the problem comes with design. Simply adding a mole or glasses is not enough to change the face of a character. You can convince me all you want that Mira is the best and you would probably be right, but I would not care who is my favorite because they all look the EXACT SAME. But other than that... pretty good cast.

Enjoyment: 8/10.
I did enjoy myself a lot while watching Master Piece The Animation. The art and animation were top notch but due to some minor problems in each category, I can't see myself giving my enjoyment anything more than an 8.

Overall: 8/10.
As of right now, Master Piece The Animation is 0.06 away from becoming the highest rater hentai on this site. Do I think this hentai is better than Mankitsu Happening? No. Do I still want to see this hentai as no.1? Yes, because it's about time Mankitsu Happening got dethroned. So watch this hentai and enjoy!

I was skeptical that a hentai titled “Masterpiece” would live up to its name, given the low standards of this genre. Turns out that skepticism was unwarranted; MP has strong production values even compared to some TV anime, and features one of the sexiest milfs to ever appear in hentai in Mira chan.

As to be expected from a S tier Milf, Mira has massive titties and an otherwise perfect body. What makes her remarkable is her dominant personality that takes charge during sex, complimented by sensual voice acting that feels convincing. This makes it very easy for the degenerate to immerse themselves into the action where the self insert male gets to plow the mature woman of his dreams after a period of teasing. This is his foster mom, btw, not his real one. So this anime manages to avoid the full on alabama incest experience, while still pandering to the forbbiden lust niche.

The other two girls, Yuna and Nina, are also hot. But they're also kinda generic as far as hentai anime waifus are concerned, so I didn't find them to be nearly as appealing as Mira who is more hard to come by. One of them is a tsun glasses girl and the other a submissive big titty waifu.

What is also hard to find is good animation quality, which this H has in spades. The sex scenes are very creative and fluid. There's lots of shot composition variety, rarely does it reuse previous frames and it never devolves into a low budget slide show. There's an interesting blur effect applied to the tits during the sex that creates the illusion that they're moving in rapid speed, very effective for making the scenes look high quality. In all this is a fantastic effort that most should appreciate from a visual standpoint.

As far as boundaries go, this show doesn't do anything that extreme. There's no rape. There's no serious NTR since they're all equally possessive of the MC. There's no loli's having sex either. The worst thing you'll see is blood once the hymen is broken and the weird X rays into the vaginal walls that hentai love to do for some reason.

Hentai has a bad reputation, for good reason, but this at least is something worth championing as an example of what the genre is capable of when executed well. Very high replay value, something I'll be revisiting for a long time.
