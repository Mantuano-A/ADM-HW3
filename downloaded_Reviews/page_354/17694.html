Mankitsu Happening is a four-part OVA series by Collaboration Works. It also happens to be the highest rated hentai on MAL, which is a title it certainly doesn't deserve.

The plot follows a guy named... fuck, does it even matter? He's a worthless character with no personality anyways. So this guy gets called up by his friend, and since his friend doesn't want him to stay a deadbeat for his whole life, he offers him a job at a manga cafe. However, the guy is reluctant to take his friend up on his offer, because of his "issue". And here we get to the guy's only character trait, which I had a very difficult time understanding. Damn, what a stud!

Despite this, the guy takes his friend's offer (on the condition he won't have to deal with many women). He shows up to work and surprise, it's practically pussy city at the manga cafe! And you know what that means...

Each episode can be broken into roughly three sections. The beginning is reserved for the plot, characters are introduced and this generally gets the story rolling. Then there's the dreadfully dull middle section, where there's no story and the sex scenes are about as interesting as curling. Finally you get to the third section, where your boredom is somewhat alleviated and your treated to some... okay sex scenes.

What about the characters? Well, there's the main guy (his name is Keiichi), he has bullshit lucky powers, which must serve some karmic purpose to make up for the fact he has no eyes. There's Keiichi's pink-haired childhood friend Kururu, who's about as stale as a week-old loaf of bread. The manga cafe's veteran employee Rei-San (aka best girl), who's a sexy kuudere milf. It's also vanilla, so there isn't really all that much objectionable content.

In closing, would I reccomend you watch Mankitsu Happening? Yes, but temper your expectations. Mankitsu Happening is a perfectly serviceable hentai, but there're far better ones out there.
