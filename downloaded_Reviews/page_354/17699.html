Youkoso! Sukebe Elf no Mori e is a fairly short hentai OVA produced by the well regarded studio T-Rex. I was recommended this series but I ultimately found it rather dissapointing.

The story is similar to the plot of most Isekai anime. The main character is transported to another world and has to help it's inhabitants. However, this is a hentai (and a very short one at that), so the story is mostly religated to the back seat. I found the story to be fine, some effort was put into it, but it ultimately didn't hold my interest or attention.

The art/animation is pretty good. The characters have smooth and consistent designs, the backdrops are colorful, and the movement is decent. Some of the characters have unbelievable proportions (I.e massive breasts), and others like the main character have bland and empty designs (no eyes). But the art is mostly good, which is to be expected from studio T-Rex.

I don't have much to say about the sound. I'm not a native Japanese speaker, so I can't judge the quality of the voice acting. The other aspects of the audio are fine, and I liked the end credits song.

The characters are really bland. They don't have many defining personality traits or goals. Besides the main character (lets simply call him generic hero) the rest of the characters are divided into two groups, the elves and the dark elves. They don't like each other but as far as I can tell they share the same goal, save their world and make more elves. I guess the regular elves are friendlier and the dark elves are more "tsundere". It doesn't really matter though since both sides are equally generic.

I didn't get much enjoyment out of this OVA series, sadly. But on the bright side each episode is only 16 minutes! Perhaps I went into this with high expectations? The way I heard this described I was under the impression this was going to be funny and creative, which it certainly isn't. 5.5/10

But overall would I recommend this? Well, if you're a fan of studio T-Rex you might enjoy this, it looks like their other shows, but I'd say it's certainly not their best work. Are you a fantasy fanatic who loves elves? If you're starved for content this might be for you, but if you're looking for something good you might want to pass. This isn't a bad hentai and I wouldn't dissuade people from watching it, however, there're plenty of other series out there more deserving of attention than this one.

2021 Edit: This review was written before it was announced that this OVA series would be picked up for two more episodes. Having seen the new episodes, I stand by most of what I said about episodes 1-2, since they still apply to episodes 3-4. However, I do want to commend the studio for the improved visuals in the new episodes. And I have bumped the score up from 5 to 5.5 as a result.

Youkoso! Sukebe Elf no Mori e is probably one of the most recognizable hentai as for years I’ve seen it on the front page of various hentai sites yet I decided not to watch it since the series was not fully out however in retrospect I am glad I never did click on it until now because I would’ve just been waiting for disappointment. Overall the hentai is unremarkable and follows the tried and true formula of ‘in another world’ + ‘hair covers eyes MC-kun’ + ‘big titty elves’ = popular hentai, it doesn’t stray from what it knows and while some may see that as a plus, I felt disappointed since a hentai needs some attribute that helps keep it in other heads long after the fap.

Story : 2

I looked high and low for a shred of substance in this hentai’s story and honestly I came up empty handed. In terms of story, we have no story as describing this hentai’s plot could lead to possibly hundreds of different answers considering how generic it is. “MC-kun gets transported to another world where he fucks elves to achieve world peace” may sound interesting at first however everything just felt like a shallow attempt to get the MC laid as many times as possible in the span of an episode. Our MC is chosen by a loli elf (because of course there’s a loli) to save her village by breeding with the inhabitants (because of course only female elves exist in this world) in order to defeat the evil dark elves (because of course there are dark elves) …. and that’s pretty much all there is to this hentai. Sukebe Elf no Mori e looks to me like a drunk man stumbling home and accidentally sticking his dick in different holes as he attempts to do so with the most expected ending, he just fucks all the elves and they all lived happily ever after. However, I will admit they are not wrong to herald MC-kun as the Messiah as it would truly take an ascended being to satisfy the legions of elves as the ‘confrontation’ between the elves and dark elves ends in MC-kun fucking the leaders of the dark elves into submission like the MEGA CHAD that he is (Yes that is what MC stands for).

Art and Animation : 8 → 5

One of the reasons I’m glad I wasn’t constantly waiting for a new episode release is in regards to the art and animation. My first impressions with this hentai were great, it looked pleasing despite the massive titties, I still enjoyed my time however since the release spanned two years the art style evolved over time and personally it suited my tastes less and less. I will probably be crucified for this however the change in art style began to resemble Master Piece which had the same attribute as the latter episodes of this hentai - motion blur. To be fair to this hentai, they were incorporating a modest amount of motion blur to the character animation and admittedly it was rather enjoyable as it helped the viewer feel the motions more vividly however as the episodes progressed it began to feel like they were just moving the motion blur slider all the way to 10 which made parts of the show unbearable to watch.

In terms of actual character design, I was pleasantly surprised with the array of characters present. Obviously there were things that I didn’t love such as the typical hentai MC with hair covering his eyes and the most ‘average’ body a person could have, however the girls were great. Not only were the elf girls distinct from each other, part of their personality was shown through their appearance with my particular favorite being Misery, bringing that raw Ara Ara energy and looking particularly vulnerable at times. I didn’t have a bad experience visually, however as they tried to get fancier with their visuals it ended up hurting them in later episodes.

Characters : 7

While visually the cast of elves were marvelous, they along with the MC did not fare so well when analyzing the substance beneath the skin. There was nothing particularly offensive among the cast, however there was nothing much to begin with. The characterization falls short due to the size of the cast, it was clear that not everyone would get their fair share of screen time so from the getgo many characters were going to come out underdeveloped however I will give some credit as during the sex (Which is necessary because its a hentai) and in any miniscule amount of dialogue, they tried their best to breathe some life into their characters.

On the other hand, our MC-kun basically stays the same throughout the entire series with a character arc as flat as a cutting board. Now traditionally I would consider this bad, sometimes we shouldn’t stick to the mould of storytelling where we need a single protagonist that holds the focus throughout a story. An example of this would be Christopher Nolan’s acclaimed Dunkirk telling not one story but rather several at the same time through the three perspectives - air, land and sea. Now that we’ve established this mindset that a story can be told in numerous ways, back to hentai we have our MC-kun who acts as a MacGuffin throughout the story. What makes this so brilliant is that our MC seems to be making proactive moves however in reality his actions are reactionary to whatever situation he gets tossed into, as in there is no goal he is striving towards, he just goes along with what is happening in the story.

To make this clearer, let’s go over a few scenes in the hentai. First and foremost, against his will he gets picked up by an elf and taken to another world where he wakes up to a thirsty elf girl slobbering all over his dick so he fucks her. He stumbles upon a dark elf so he saves her and fucks her. The elves need babies and ask him to fuck them so he fucks them. He stumbles upon an elf and attempts to protect her leading him into a fuck war with the dark elves. While at first glance, it looks like he is taking action however MC-kun has no motivation whatsoever and is just reacting to the random events thrown at him which comes off as lazy storytelling and his ultimate victory over the dark elves leading to peace can all but certainly be chalked up to luck. What’s brilliant however is if we see him as the MacGuffin of the plot where there really isn’t a true protagonist we end up with a rare experience allowing us to see how the plot progresses from the point of view of the MacGuffin. What happens seems reactionary however since he is the tool used to drive the plot, we can forgive this as the so-called “real protagonists” of the story are in fact the many characters we deem as side characters since they are the ones taking action to save their population and end the war.

All in all, I still cannot forgive that many side characters were underdeveloped and that the world felt so empty, feeling like a movie set specifically tailored for this particular set of characters and nothing existed outside that space. However, if we understand that the story is more akin to an experimental work which places us in the shoes of the movie MacGuffin then the blow to character quality is lessened and we can appreciate the experience we are granted.
