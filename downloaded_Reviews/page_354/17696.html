Rance 01 is a hentai with both good looking "plot" as well as actual plot. Hard to believe, but it actually pulls off a story about a man on a journey to rescue one girl quite well despite some very nice looking sex scenes breaking it up.

This hentai follows a man named Rance. He's a pervert who owns a female slave and dreams of having sex with many different women. Despite all of the problems he has from that first statement, he's actually not a terrible person (except to his slave, Siru, who he's a pretty big dick to... literally as well). Fortunately this hentai takes a lighthearted approach to most things with good comedy and a colorful art-style that help lighten up the mood in some of it's darker moments. Granted this hentai does have rape as well as a slightly-sexualized torture and blatantly implied death scene at the end of episode 3, so be warned that this may be comedic, but it is not a vanilla hentai.

But that's not what really matters in this. As good as the plot and comedy might be, that's not what most people are thinking about watching this for. The sex scenes are still what matters most, and they deliver extremely well. The best part about them, in my opinion, is that they get better as the episodes progress (most likely due to the success of the hentai allowing them to spend more money on the animation). It was very nice to see episode 3 be spectacular even compared to the other two episodes. Rance 01 does a great job of not repeating shots over and over. It uses quite a range of angles and plenty of motion in it's sex scenes that will keep you very interested.

My only real complaint with the sex scenes is how few there are each episode. Each episode has one sex scene between Rance and Siru (the third episode's scene is the best scene so far in my opinion) and a scene between Rance and some other girl that he happens to come across in his journey. His relationships with each of these girls are nice to watch as he usually does help them despite making a couple of them have sex unwillingly at first. But since there's only two real sex scenes per episode, the rest is filled up with some surprisingly well-animated action for a hentai and some plot. Most of the plot takes up about half of the total episode time, so if you don't care about that stuff, just skip from sex scene to sex scene.

Rance 01, is a great-looking hentai though, and just keeps getting better and better. The animation is very fluid and the characters' positions and the main character actually having a personality will keep you drawn in during the sex scenes. Still, avoid it if you hate the idea of a man having a female slave who he treats like shit or just skip to episode 3 and stop after the second sex scene.

Rance is a well-known name in hentai history and really sparked something different in me when I first watched it. It has quite a long run time of 4 episodes and is one of the most story driven hentai I have watched which strives to break the societal norms and expectations that has plagued hentai quality checks. Many people assume hentai is just 'porn' or 'fap material' but Rance proves that a balance can be struck between story and sex which makes the material feel whole and not devoid of character motivation.

Story : 10

In the essence of preserving the first time watch experience of the viewer I will refrain from major spoilers and mainly cover introductory plot that you can glean from the synopsis and a broad overview of what makes Rance so enjoyable. I had watched Rance long ago and only just rewatched it, remarkably I remembered every plot point and many of the characters which feels so rare considering the large amount of hentai I consume which do little to leave an impression on me and fade in obscurity before long but Rance manages to keep fresh in my mind with a simple yet effective way of storyteling. The story covers our hero Rance, who is far from your run-off-the-mill blank slate hentai MC, a great warrior who has been tasked with finding a missing girl aided by his poorly treated and bumbling slave Sill. The great thing about Rance is that it is has a 'true' plot, meaning that there is something happening and isn't just there to facilitate our hero's sexual exploits which sadly is not so common in most hentai we see produced. The sex scenes are short in the grand scheme of the episode, we have two per episode on average and they are relative short but it doesn't matter because most people skip through long and arduous sex scenes so there is not really much point of them being long for the purposes of fapping. This is so great as it feels like we're watching something meant to be watched and not just fapped to, the longer story scenes provide motivation and background as to why Rance is able to fuck these girls giving us the key to this show : The fap is earned. You have to watch longer story scenes, which are entertaining, and you get these short sex scenes which you do not need to skip through which overall creates a better experience that no show has really been able to replicate.

Art : 8

Rance has great artwork, distinguishing between characters and serving to enhance personalities by manifesting these character traits into tangible physical features. The sidecharacter also have plenty of detail and are unique in design, the environment is drawn well and makes the world feel lived in which adds to the feeling of Rance being a 'whole' product. Furthermore we are treated to 'sakuga' fight scenes in the latter episodes which just makes me marvel at the quality of this show even more.

Sound : 7

The sound does not take away from the show but is nothing special, we have the standard set of sound effects and is adequate for the purposes of the show.

Character : 8

People may argue that Rance characterwise is not up to par with the story but this seems like a completely false statement to me. The main argument I feel is brought up is that Rance himself seems like your standard pervy MC but people who argue this miss the whole point of Rance. He is a caricature of the pervert, an embodiment of horniness which is the main driver in comedy for the show. He does not develop as a character because that isn't what he is supposed to do, he is 'the greatest warrior' and he will get whatever he wants while the other characters develop around him and comically are oblivious to his perverted habits while revering him as a hero. The bulk of character development comes from Rance's episode flings where within the episode we see how he establishes and develops relations with each girl as well as how they change how they treat him. All this however pales in comparison to the largest show spanning character arc which is with Rance's poor slave Sill which covers her major character shift as well as how she interacts with Rance.

I think Rance is a once in a blue moon sort of show with actual content instead of just a womb full of cum. Furthermore Rance doesn't really take any shortcuts to get the popularity it has attained such as shocking the audience with multiple outrageous tags and can be argued to be a more mellow hentai besides the OVA which I will do a short review on later. We have standard rape and hints of torture but pales in comparison to the extreme content shown in the OVA so even for the vanilla viewers it will not do what Euphoria has done to me by any stretch of the imagination. Rance is a universally loved show and unlike most popular hentai it is not 'infamous' in any way which is rather rare for hentai. I give Rance my highest recommendations with two faps an episode and one episode a session because letting the story sink in over multiple sessions guarantees a great experience
