I had hoped to review this series first, but alas alack, I have exams until the end of this month. I actually had an exam today and yesterday, so while I had time to watch it since it was only 20 minutes or so, I didn't have time to cut into my revision schedule to make this review. It's the weekend now, so I finally have time to keep going with this hentai review thing. You know the drill by now: I'm a girl so I'm sorry if that affects my review in a bad way. Moving on.

In a nutshell, it was pretty awesome. I feel like it just got everything it attempted so very right, and it was also an original setup for a very common theme. This is revenge-oriented, and so the protagonist will not show mercy. If you want something nice and guilt-free, this isn't for you. It doesn't go as far as, say, Euphoria in the guilt factor, but it manages to sexualize something that you know is wrong and, although it gives explanation, you know the rape isn't justified. And yet I still found it to be very hot and interesting to watch, which I must commend it for. I loved the first episode, so I have to pray that it doesn't pull a "Hime-sama Gentei!" and fuck up all the hotness at the end.

Story was surprisingly decent for a hentai. Our protagonist- no, not protagonist. I'll go with "main character" instead this time, considering everything. Anyway, our MC wants revenge on his father after he abandoned him and his mother to have two daughters with another woman. It's an interesting motive behind the MC and, although it doesn't manage to give him any depth, it actually manages to hold the hentai together well and justifies his actions somewhat. The sob story does draw you in, as it's the first scene of the hentai. You think "Oh, ok. We got some plot here." and it only takes 5 minutes of plottage to make you understand the MC and the two girls a bit, and since the backstory was only short, it didn't bog the hentai down at all. Rather, it improved the quality of the series and gave me a whole lot of patience and respect for the series. Obviously it won't be the best story in hentai, but it's definitely above average for hentai. In order to get his revenge, he is given a magical stopwatch that can stop time and let him do whatever he wants as time is stopped. This is used to create a "helpless girls" storyline, but God, is it used well. I partially like this because there are only two girls; none of this harem bullshit. As I've mentioned in previous reviews, I also love hentai with magical items in them because they are always original and full of potential. Bible Black and Dorei Usagi to Anthony are examples of hentai that used magical items well. I also liked the way they explained how he didn't just go straight into the Girls (literally); he tested the watch first and saw how it worked. He looks up a girl's skirt (like we all would) and he freezes a crow (Lain-style). It's a little addition, but it makes me respect the series a whole lot more.

Art was very nice also- not quite up to Lovely x Cation standards, but definitely on par with Renai Fuyou Gakuha. I really hope the direction art in hentai is going in hentai continues; they're really lovely to look at as of late. The animations don't look choppy like they tend to, and the animation loops were quite smooth, in that the place where they looped wasn't obvious like it usually is. The character designs of the girls were absolutely gorgeous, and they looked beautiful. The MC was obviously plainer, but he wasn't ugly by a long-shot, which I also loved because guys in hentai are getting too ugly lately. I know that I'm probably the only girl watching them and so the only person it affects, but it still upsets me when they do that (partially because I feel sorry for the girls!). The only thing about the character design of the MC was that I sometimes felt that the length they made bangs in order to cover his eyes completely was a bit forced, but that's it. It just looked a bit unnatural. So was the size of his member, but there's no point in mentioning that!

Sound, in terms of voice acting, was standard except for the MC, because the craziness of his character and how his childhood had unhinged him really came across in his voice. It really stood out. The girls' voices were cute and the acting for them was also very good, but it's the extra sprinkles on top we get from the MC that really made his voice memorable. Just his tone of voice was interesting compared to other male protagonists. It dances up and down, and he sounds very eccentric. It also makes him likeable somewhat. He's an asshole, but he's an interesting one! The amount of emotion he had when he was speaking about his father was also notable. Another thing that I noticed was how the sound effects to make the dialogue sound like he's "doing stuff" while speaking was very good (e.g. muffled voice and slurping noises) and he delivers a surprising amount of lines as he's licking and sucking things, which sounded awesome.

The highlight was definitely when he mimics the younger sister's moe voice whilst she's frozen by the stopwatch. It was like when Akio Furukawa mimicked Nagisa's voice in Clannad. It made me laugh out loud! The dialogue is almost completely a monologue by the MC as he does stuff, and the things he says aren't the same-old same-old, which kept the whole series fresh and interesting for me. The only thing he says that is heard in all hentai was actually the impression of the sister, where he grabs her jaw as she's frozen and moves it like she's talking, saying "But it's so big! I'm scared!" in a high voice before switching back to normal to say "It's fine if you want to say that, you know." and the whole time she's froze so can't speak. It was a very funny scene that'll stick in my memory for a while! His whole crazy demeanour was like if you were being raped by Okabe Rintarou- and for that, it automatically gets a thumbs up from me. His maniacal laughter in between thrusts was also really fun to watch. I'm going to hell.

Yeah, it's just one guy having his way with two girls; but it's the way this series delivers it that makes it memorable. Freezing the girls during foreplay and then unfreezing them mid-thrust was a very cool way of doing time manipulation in hentai. The way they are confused and are at the same time trying to save the other sister is really interesting to watch and I believe that this series is a perfect example of how to use a magical item in hentai. The whole episode is very fresh and I haven't seen such an unhinged male MC in all my time watching hentai, except for Kei in Euphoria. I can still hear him saying "This is it! This is the face!". This guy isn't memorable because he's creepy as hell when he gets his motor runnin' (looking at you, Kei) but because he's different. This episode is different. I would wholeheartedly recommend that you watch this first episode because of the great production value, fascinating MC and the GORGEOUS sisters. You're betraying your loins if you don't check this out.

I love time stop so much, it’s one of my favorite genres of hentai where it has so many different ways to go. The worlds of these time stop hentai and doujin don’t have to be built upon strict rules and no two rule sets of timestop are ever the same. Time stop is special as it can introduce a plausible and relatable supernatural aspect into either the most fantastical worlds to the more mundane settings without seeming out of place. Everyone wishes they could control time, it is a universal desire as for mortals time is finite and feels extremely scarce the older you grow. As I grow up, I find myself thinking more and more as if I am running out of time so time stop has become so much more relatable to me. While they never break the baseline rules of time stop, they never bring out the full potential of the genre and while there were some cute things done such as bring people’s heads out of stopped time in order for him to force them to watch him rape their friends, it didn’t satisy me as I rarely get to see this gimmick utilized in my hentai. I have grown attached to time stop, much more so than any other genre, and that is why I can’t say that Gakuen de Jikan yo Tomare does the genre any justice.

Story : 2

To stay this hentai had a plot would be extremely generous as it basically crams in all of the plot into the first 5 minutes of the first episode in a four episode series making up a measly twenty-fourth of the series. The story centers around the illegitimate child of a powerful man who was cast aside and forced to watch his mother die after being denied any form of help from his biological father, seeing the man’s own two daughters being cared for and loved while he was seen as nothing more than a stranger to his own family. Filled with vengeance he vows to take revenge and ruin his father’s prestigious academy where his own half sisters attend aided by a time stopping watch he is given by a mysterious woman. That about sums it up as from this point on it becomes a complete fuckfest in both the sense that he fuck a bunch of girls indiscriminantly and that the story falls apart past this point where each episode is given half-assed dialogue at the start or end to tie in the next episode and give the illusion that some type of progress is being made.

What truly sucks is that it gave me a lot of anticipation of a good story as there was motivation and emotion conveyed in the backstory however afterwards all pretenses of storytelling went out the window. The first episode is decent as he plays with the emotions of both sisters, pulling them in and out of time to see their sister getting fucked relentlessly which was quite commendable however in every other episode there was no more of that emotion or painful drive behind the rape. The ending was equally disappointing as all the buildup and talk from the beginning of the show was all for naught as the quest of our “hero” was to take revenge upon his father by destroying his crowning achievement, his academy. While we see him ravage the school by raping indiscriminately, we never get our conclusion as to how his life or those around him continued after these events transpired leaving the story seeming half-baked.

Art and Animation : 7

Honestly nothing much to say here, it really is just your typical hentai with eye covered MC-kun and a bunch of girls with arguably reasonable body proportions which is always a plus. I do like how they play with physics as the bodily fluids fly through the air frozen in time however that’s really where variety ends. Our MC-kun is in the blandest outfit possible, the typical hentai protagonist donning his black pants, white collared shirt and eyes covered by hair while the girls were in school uniforms or swimsuits. Character design for the females was flat as a tsundere where many girls felt as if they were from other scenes when they were new characters entirely.

The facial expressions of the girls basically trampled on the whole idea of mindbreak where these girls were raped once and instead of showing the faces of trauma or shock, they looked mindbroken. Not to discount the horrors of rape however the idea of the mindbreak to me is that after prolonged abuse these girls would lose their identity, their soul and their will to the rape leaving them as hollow shells. This just didn’t look right to me.

Characters : 3

I will give kudos when kudos are due so while this hentai doesn’t get a flat out 0, don’t let me lead you astray thinking there was any semblance of character development. The 3 comes purely from the opening setup where we establish quite a dark and realistic backstory for our MC’s revenge story however that’s it. None of the girls get any character development since we never see them after each episode and there is no resolution which means we don’t get any payoff for the overarching storyline which was the entire pull for me to finish this hentai as I thought at the very least the evil father would get his comeuppance however we are left with disappointment, the overwhelming post-fap depression and a limp dick

Disappointment : 10

If you or someone you know is having too good of a life, then this hentai is special for you so you can get a taste of what we normal people like to call disappointment. Like a kid unwrapping a PS4 box on Christmas day only to open it up and see it’s filled with pictures of mind broken Japanese school girls, this show is right for you. I have lost several job prospects due to COVID-19, I’m graduating university this month at home where I haven’t set foot on campus in months and I can’t travel home till God knows when however this show has got to be the greatest disappointment I’ve experienced all year so go fuck yourself Gakuen de Jikan yo Tomare, you ruined multiple meat beater sessions.

Enjoyment : 0

Don’t watch this hentai, it really isn’t worth the time, blood, swear or milky tears to fap to this piece of shit. Hentai is one of, if not the most, interesting genres of fapping fuel with the highest degree of freedom given the medium of animation and yet this show was able to show me the most boring time in all my years of fapping.
