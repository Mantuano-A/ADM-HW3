First things first: this is certainly fap-able material!

However, the story itself is very, very bland...
A young man living next to three milfs suddenly finds himself at the center of a frenzied fuck-fest!

Spurred on from their lonely, partner-missing lives, these three housewives were apparently wooed by the boy next-door after he helped them take out their trash. No other reasoning could possibly begin to explain why they would break their marital vows... They simply couldn't stop themselves after the MC's birthday! (If you desire more substance to your smut, then this fails to deliver any semblance of satisfying motive; sorry) Although, seeing as to how this is a hentai, their love-making was simply inevitable!

Mediocre lip-syncing coupled with lackluster animation can be found in spades as you watch this non-stop ride on the "home-wrecking express."
The art itself is not terrible and the money-shots are probably the best-done thing in this hentai.

The characters themselves are generic and repetitive but still likable. The voice-acting was passable but nothing really to be impressed by. And, all of the women are basically the embodiment of someones vision of the ideal housewife with big-tits, wide hips, and a slim waist. Each one is slightly different from the last, but they are all still pretty much the same in every way... While not necessarily bad, this lends itself to having only one flavor at this mother-loving party.

What I found most enjoyable was the fact that each sex scene was tastefully created.
The variety of locations and poses kept things fresh & interesting!

All in all, this hentai felt like it was made for those who fantasize about the perfect milf: lonely from work-busy husbands and desperate to be bred...

3.5/10 - Did watch again.

Okusama wa Moto Yariman is a two-part OVA series by Circle Tribute. It's a fairly standard vanilla hentai about milfs.

So, what's the story? Well, the story is all about this young guy named Yuuto, who happens to live next door to three busty milfs. He helps them out by taking care of their kids, and other stuff like that. But on his birthday, he takes his relationship with them to a whole new level.

Okay, so it's clearly a pretty standard vanilla hentai plot. There's not a whole lot of substance to it. The characters hang out, they eat, they go shopping for clothes, stuff like that. It gets the job done, but it's pretty bare-bones.

The art/animation is okay, but it's also somewhat lacking. The characters look a little odd from some shots, and their faces are drawn quite strange for some scenes. The animation is also fairly limited. There're some scenes that are barely animated and only consist of several still shots. These are quite jarring, since most of the other scenes are fully animated. But apart from that, the art/animation is mostly tolerable. The voice actors also do a fine job at conveying their characters.

Speaking of characters... Besides Yuuto (who's pretty much your standard male protagonist), there're also three female leads. These are Yuuto's mature neighbors, and their names currently escape me (I probably should've written them down before I started writing this review). But perhaps that just goes to show how forgettable they are. They have barely any personality, and are almost interchangeable. I think my favorite is the brunette one.

Overall, Okusama wa Moto Yariman is a decent, if forgettable, OVA series. It has some decent scenes and okay art, but it's not something I'm really going to actively remember either. 6/10

So, would I reccomend you watch this OVA series? Do you like milfs? If so, then my answer is yes. And to everyone else, sure. Just don't expect anything amazing.
