Chu shite agechau starts off slow and becomes better and better as it progresses.

This title begins with a funny scene starring the main male character, Akira, jerking off to a video and being interrupted by his big sister Yuu who offers to service him. The plot then revolves around the boy being clueless about puberty and sex, and being taught on the subject by different women during various lovemaking sessions. Akira is kind of a blank slate character, one that viewers should usually be able to identify with easily. I personally felt that he was used as a sex object the entire time. The tone is comedic and delivers a few chuckles. The story isn’t to be taken seriously and so the focus is on the H scenes.

Save for height and colors, the female characters’ bodies have many similarities, big breasts for their size and a slightly more slender lower body.

Yuu is a cute, busty redhead whose role is to take care of her brother. She is caring and collected, and doesn’t seem to have any moral barriers when it comes to getting involved in incestuous sex. She sleeps with Akira in order to “help” him with his “treatment”.

Kimika is the doctor who examines Akira and recommends he gets laid as often as possible. Her appearance and personality is that of a mature and professional woman. She wasn’t very interesting to me at first, partly because the sex scene she stars in stems from a lengthy, non-erotic context and because Akira remains awkwardly still all the time. The second half of their scene gets better though, she looks sexier when dressed in casual wear later on and she is ultimately bolder and more lustful than Yuu.

Sana seems to be a friend of Akira. She too lacks experience in bed, has long blond hair, a slimmer, tighter frame and comes off as a clumsy, tsundere yet lovely girl. Her cute expressions and curves of more credible size made her my favorite even though she has less screen time.

Nozomu is a nurse who makes a brief appearance by joining the fun in the final H scene. There isn’t much to say about her.

The art and shading is fine. It’s what one would expect of a 2013 release’s standards. The quality of animation varies: some acts are detailed, smooth and have a good amount of frames, others are much simpler.

There are 10 sex scenes and they feature:
1: (Yuu) handjob, facial, paizuri + blowjob.
2: (Kimika) blowjob, cowgirl position, internal view, creampie.
3: (Kimika) blowjob (brief).
4: (Yuu) masturbation, licking, vaginal penetration.
5: (Kimika) from behind position.
6: (Yuu) blowjob, from behind position.
7: (Sana) blowjob, first time, doggie, cowgirl position.
8: (Kimika, Sana) threesome, cowgirl position, anal fingering, licking, doggie.
9: (Yuu, Kimika, Sana), triple blowjob + anal play, licking, fondling, paizuri + blowjob.
10: (All) vaginal penetration, licking, breasts grab, varied positions, anal fingering.

Several blowjob and handjob scenes also include nicely animated ball play, something I believe we don’t see enough in hentai anime and therefore is very welcomed. Most penetration scenes also end with the boy cumming inside the girl, and female characters often keep their underwear during lovemaking.

In the first episode, the sex scenes seemed to lack some spice, some more emotional involvement from the characters. The absence of any moral questions, romantic feelings and the naïveté of the main characters are somewhat disconcerting. This is the only way I can describe it: it lacks feel. Granted, Akira has absolutely no idea, no knowledge about sex and intimacy and is taught about it all throughout the movie. I suppose the staff’s aim was to appeal to the viewers’ first time interest in these subjects, but one would still expect the other characters to be emotionally involved beyond the mere pleasure of giving and receiving sexual stimulation.

It all changes in the second episode though. The girls are depicted in a more erotic fashion. Sex scenes have a noticeable increase in roughness and lewdness. The characters express more emotions and Akira is more proactive. Sex sounds effects are not subtle anymore. Breasts jiggle more often. These changes triggered more interest on my part right from the get-go. Note that this episode also features nearly non-stop sex. I have to say this one is far more enjoyable than the first.

At times, there is censorship over the female crotch even when the girl’s panties are on, which felt unnecessary and silly. The amount of censorship varies: it is usually small but some close-up shots feature larger pixels.

This is the second time I review a hentai title produced by PoRo, and I’ve noticed that in both titles music and sound effects are usually soft and subtle. The voice actresses do a good job and didn’t sound annoying. However, in a few scenes, the girls talk with their mouth full from servicing the boy or without any lips movement. That didn’t felt realistic at all and broke immersion. I suppose we should blame the animators for this.

I have a few favorite moments:
Kimika’s frog-like position when doing it with Akira in the bathroom. I thought it was funny yet original and arousing.
Sana’s first time in a very nicely-drawn doggy style position.
And the two final group sex scenes are a nice, rewarding ending to the movie.

Needless to say, if you are irked by incestuous relations you should probably stay away from this title. I personally enjoyed Chu shite agechau fairly, the second episode a lot more than the first. Many aspects could have been better but nothing is really bad either. Maybe the mood the production team was going for in the first episode simply wasn’t my cup of tea, but its H scenes are still decent.

I’d recommend this title for those of you who want a hentai that is light in tone and who enjoy watching female characters take the lead and service a guy (in the first episode). The second episode should please any kind of viewer. If you are new to the hentai genre I’d say it is perfect to watch as an introductory title.

Final words: more doctors should prescribe that kind of treatment.
