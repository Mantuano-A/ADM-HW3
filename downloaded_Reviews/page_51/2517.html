Most likely still stuck in stone for several thousand years, full of statues who are angry and aren't satisfied with themselves. It came as quite a surprise when I saw an advert on YouTube featuring Senku advertising Snickers bars, as if 2020 couldn't get any more weirder.

This ad features Senku and Japanese comedian Kouki Kusanagi as they spoof on the series by replacing the Miracle Fluid with a chocolate bar. It's kinda obvious to know how well Snickers is gonna sell these chocolate bars when they collaborate with a fairly mainstream anime.

Also, for Senku to create something that closely resembles a Snickers bar, he would require: milk chocolate (sugar, cocoa butter, chocolate, skim milk, lactose, milkfat, soy lecithin, artificial flavor), peanuts, corn syrup, sugar, palm oil, skim milk, lactose, salt, egg whites, artificial flavor. This would make for a weird but slightly interesting spin-off series. And if he wants to go full Scottish, he can batter them in some sort of flour and deep fry them in oil (really fatty, but quite delicious).

My only concern is that the comedian should've been naked when he got de-petrified, meaning that a Snickers bar breaks all known lore and logic of the Dr. Stone universe.

Overall, 7/10.

Let me just begin with a confession, I’ve rewatched this anime so many times that I lost count. It’s just… so fucking good.

Story : 10/10
Senku shows off the powers of the snicker, a chocolate candy bar crammed with peanuts, caramel and nougat then coated with milk chocolate. He feeds it to a man who is stuck in stone because of the petrification beam. The snicker breaks the stone and frees the man.

Art : 10/10
It’s just outstanding. Super detailed, you could even compare it to stuff like Berserk and JoJos. The Snicker is super detailed and just looking at it makes me drool like crazy. Gosh even by thinking about the snicker makes me so fucking hungry.

Sound : 10/10
The soundtrack is absolutely godlike and it surpasses any soundtrack made before. The only thing we can compare it to is the Boku no Pico soundtrack, both BNPs and the Dr Stone Snicker soundtrack really fit the animation and story, they’re both very emotional and both made me cry. But the dr stone snicker soundtrack just surpasses the one of Boku No Pico.
Also, the voice actors are absolutely astonishing. Especially the man stuck in stone, after he gets freed from the stone he moans loudly in happiness while eating the rest of the snicker bar.

Characters : 10/10
Senku ; amazing character with an amazing backstory, voice actor, character design, IQ and attitude. He invented the snicker bar, we must praise him.
The man stuck in stone ; super tragic character. He has an amazing voice character and has some very memorable lines. His character design is so memorable, I’ve never seen anything like it before.

Enjoyment : 10/10
I’ve never seen such an exiting, deep and most of all emotional anime in any anime, manga, movie, book or comic before. I’ll be rewatching this anime throughout my whole life until the day I die.

Overall : 10/10
I definitely recommend watching this anime, it’ll change your perspective on life.
