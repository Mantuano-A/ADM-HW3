Based on the Adult PC game of the same name, this title puts the highlight on the Netorare or NTR genre on the spotlight. This title was practically animated to market the PC game with out a doubt.

Long story short we have the main couple staring out taking a trip to the male protagonists home village. Little that female lead knew is that she was being set up as bait so that the male protagonist can reunite with his childhood sweetheart. Who was now married to the father of the male lead. The father is also the main village ritual leader. The ritual involves an orgy with either one of the female lead characters.

Things take a turn for the worst when the wife of the protagonist becomes a village sex slave. While the main guy tries to elope with his childhood friend, which turned out to be a set up. He gets ambushed and knocked out, barely escaping with his life.

After returning to Tokyo, he tries to contact the village with no luck. Then later gets a video in the mail showing both his "wife" and childhood friend participating in a "ritual". With both now claiming loyalty to the village as sex slaves. Even the childhood lead saying that she was ordered to pretend to be interested in leaving with him.

The story ends with the main guy hopelessly watching the orgy on this television.

Great animation quality, character dubbing, and music. Greatly captures the ominous and lustful mood of the show. This title desperately needs a sequel.

This by far is the best NTR show I have come accross. Unfortunately I have yet to play the original PC game.
