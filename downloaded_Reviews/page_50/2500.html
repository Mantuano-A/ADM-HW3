No Doubt In Us is an underrated gem.

Normally, when I see a manhua based anime, I assume that I won't like it. Cause that is what happens most of the times. Most of those animes are fast paced. So, usually I prefer the manhua, as the animated versions don't live up to the expectations.

But No Doubt In Us is different in that aspect. It follows the manhua really well & because of the animation, some scenes even look better compared to the manhua. This anime has-

1. Really catchy opening & ending
2. The animation is a bit different & I liked it
3. The characters are well written & lovable
4. The comedy is good. And it made me laugh at scenes
5. The lead couple & their interactions are cute

If you like romantic comedy with a historical twist, you will like it.

NO DOUBT IN US review (contains spoilers)

No Doubt In Us it's a cute comedy romance donghua in which the Emperor (smart but weak) and the Empress (strong but dumb) switch bodies after falling inside a lake. They now have to find a way to return in their original body but it's not easy as it seems, after various attempts, they decide to tell the grandmaster. While looking for a solution, they act and do each other's tasks/jobs, because of that they will start understanding each other more and the struggle that both had to go through.



(This paragraph contains spoilers) The emperor finds out that many concubines were always trying to get her in trouble and he never stood up for her. He always believed the concubines instead, and she would be the one getting in trouble because of lies. The people that he trusted turned out to be fake and some even wanted him dead, on the other hand, the people he didn't trust turn out to be the most loyal ones that would have even died to defend him._



__Story - 7__

The story is pretty good but at the same time is just another romance/historical donghua in which the characters swap bodies. If you enjoy the kind of stories that take place in a historical setting in which the main characters hate each other then you'll love this one.



__Animation - 6__

If you don't mind CGI then you'll be fine watching this. I think it fits the manhua art style perfectly.



__Characters/Development - 9__

I really loved multiple characters and some others were just annoying but after all, they were supposed to be annoying.



Yu Xu - A strong woman whose family works in the military. She is pretty dumb, but she is nice and cute.

Jinyun Xiao's brother - Childish, loyal, dumb but can be smart when he wants to. He is a cinnamon roll.

Grandmaster - Loyal, smart, and serious about his job



The development's amazing in my opinion. The two leading characters improve a lot because of the events that reveal the truth.



Enjoyment- 10

I loved this donghua just like I loved the manhua, maybe because I love historical romance but still.

Again, this is a really cute story because we get to see the development from hate to love and what caused the hate in the first place. [Spoilers] The empress always loved the emperor since she was a kid and she even wanted to marry him. When they got married, the emperor thought she detested him because of misunderstanding so he never paid attention to her and would prefer to be around the concubines, the people seeing the emperor didn't respect her would take advantage of that and treat her like shit.

The soundtrack's beautiful, I really love listening to the OP.

This is a hidden gem, how have this donghua fall under my radar for so long, maybe people don't really like 3d cgi. Overall great donghua, I loved the characters, they are super cute and relatable. The main couple's story is very romantic, I did not expect it at all. I watched this with low expectations but oh boy, I fell in love and read the entire manhwa. The characters are lovable and the couple's skills and personality complete each other. The plot is pretty decent too, keeps me going and I can't wait for the rest of the episodes to release! I don't mind the 3d cgi at all, in fact it's beautifully made and their expressions are beautifully shown, I tried to find any obvious flaws with the animation and couldn't, it was really made with care. I have rewatched the whole series twice as of this writing, makes me happy as the story is very wholesome.
