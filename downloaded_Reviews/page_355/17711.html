"WARNING THIS REVIEW CONTAINS SPOILERS"

Forcefully moving the girl's body through hypnotism or mind control that turns them into horny girls is too damn ordinary. Instead let's 'overwrite some of their common sense a little bit', let's overwrite code in their memory that raping a guy with a reasoning that having sex with a girl that a guy doesn't love is the greatest shame for that guy (well, yes but actually no). Which is utterly nonsense. The girls think that they are bullying him to the highest degree but it's actually the reality is that they are actually being bamboozled by his 9000 IQ play.

I have enjoyed the usage of advanced mind control and hypnosis tactics the MC have used. I also like the fact that he has only took revenge on his perpetrators only. It was never shown that he has also hypnotized their mothers and sisters or his other classmates or teachers or literally every female in his world. For me this is refreshing because almost every other MC would bang the whole school or neighborhood.

This is overall well adapted, but my only complaint is that there is one panel in the doujin's final chapters: where the protagonist kissed the main heroine with an angry face, wasn't shown in the last episode of the anime. It's not that big of a deal and it's just one panel anyway, but it would be great if they've included it also.

9/10 - I highly recommend this if you want advanced usage of mind control and hypnosis tactics.

Look, this was animated well, the voice actors did a good job and I'm sure fans of the genre will get something out of it, but the ideas behind this one are unpleasant to say the least.
