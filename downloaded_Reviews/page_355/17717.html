The summer hentai (July and August) line up has been (for the most part) extremely disappointing. Besides from continuations for pre-existing series (such as Master Piece the Animation and Seikatsu Shuukan) there is really nothing that peaked my interest for these two upcoming months of hentai besides: "Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai", which will be the series we are reviewing today.

Story: 2/10.
When watching the previews of this series and hearing that it would only be 15 minutes per episode I already made the conclusion that this hentai would have BARELY any story. Was I right in assuming this? More than right actually. Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai has nothing for the story As good as the art and directing may be this hentai has one of the worst examples of story. Look, I am fine with there being a non-existent premise, so many present day hentai's do it. But at least try to focus on pacing. There is not a moment in the hentai that is used as a means to give a short break in between the fu*ks. And for some that does not affect their enjoyment, but too much of a good thing can reduce its value, which is exactly the case here.

Art and Animation: 9/10.
The art in Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai is great. The drawings and backgrounds have great details to them. My only gripe with the art is the coloring. I am sure this is not the first time most of you have seen the color aesthetic. You know the coloring that has this 'indescribable shininess' to it and 'immense contrast'. Look I love that type of art style, but I think the biggest flaw with that art style is that when scenes take place in any time that is not day, it just comes off as 'weird'. Other then that tho the art is great. The animation is superb, I have no flaws with it, and as for the choreography of the shmex scenes, they defiantly don't disappoint.

Sound: 9/10.
The sound for Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai is great. All the VAs do a superb job like usual and the bg music used is great for the atmosphere and art style as a whole. I will however say that the bg music is a bit too loud for some scenes which brings my overall score down.

Character: 7/10.
The characters in Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai are what they are. They are your typical slutty sister leads. I do praise 'Masterpiece the Animation' for it's character because on the surface these two shows would seem like they have a similar cast in terms of personality but Masterpiece tries to add more quirks to the cast. Now there is nothing wrong with the cast for Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai, they get the job done, and get it done good. Megumi in my eyes has one of the best dark skin designs in hentai (Can't say the same about Ayami as she is too generic). But if you are going into this hentai for character personalities and development, don't expect too much from this.

Enjoyment: 9/10.
I did have my flaws with Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai, it had practically no story and the characters could have been close to perfection if they tried a little bit more. But with that being said I still fucking enjoyed every second of this hentai. When I make this enjoyment section I turn off my reviewer mentality and base my score strictly off of what I came to enjoy in the first place, 'The Hentai'. And Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai completely delivers as a hentai with superb art design and animation to go around!

Overall: 6/10.
As a reviewer of hentai I found myself having many flaws with it. But as an overall hentai, Ecchi na Onee-chan ni Shiboraretai is great. As of the upload date of this review the hentai has a MAL score of 7.35, with over 1500 members, which I think it completely deserves. As a reviewer I would say be vary about going into this one, but as someone who loves hentai, the show is a must watch!
