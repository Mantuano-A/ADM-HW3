Very enjoyable with a great female cast, good enough story, and good art. It's a good try for anyone who likes hentai at all.

Koiito Kinebi The Animation strikes that great balance of heartwarming sex scenes while also filling it with two other side stories that will give you hilarious and cute characters to gawk at.

Technically speaking, this hentai focuses on three stories, one of which is like the "main story" while the rest are sort of side stories that have no relation to it. These two do not necessarily drag the main story down too much as it is a straightforward story, to begin with. You have a boy and girl that fall in love, meet her mother, and then get married. That is about as simple as you can get. I would have loved more delicious sex scenes between the two, especially with the girl's MILF of a mother. But for what they are worth they are adorable and sexy. The sex scenes outside of the main story are just as hot and sexy. One of them involving cuties wearing Santa outfits is a definite highlight.

The animation and artwork are top-notch. It doesn't look too budget, and all of the characters look fantastic. As with hentai standards, it does not do anything groundbreaking, but it does its job well. I'm always happy whenever hentai does the moe/chibi transformations that you often see in traditional comedy anime nowadays. There are some hilarious visuals to the characters that happen to lighten the mood.

The story of this is cute. Although it is simple even by hentai standards, it does not try to be anything but. The main girl Kanako, played by the legendary Hitomi, is too damn adorable for words. She comes across pure and innocent to raging slut when she wants her man's cock for her tasteful pleasure. Not to mention her mother is the hottest girl in the entire hentai. The girls in the side stories are all just as cute and sexy; oddly enough, they are all blondes. All of them provide the right balance between cute moe girls to hot cock sluts.

It's about as vanilla as soft serve vanilla ice cream you would get at your local Dairy Queen. If you want to fap away to something that provides some heartwarming romance, this one takes the cake, vanilla cake that is.
