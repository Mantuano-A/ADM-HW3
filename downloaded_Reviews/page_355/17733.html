I love wholesome hentai as much as the next guy, there really is no better postfap feeling than finishing a vanilla hentai. Instead of being in absolute horror knowing that you were extremely turned on by girls being raped, strangled or generally abused, you got to watch two characters grow close and explore their sexuality in a consenting relationship which is always a breath of fresh air in the sea of degeneracy. Although it doesn’t live up to another related one-shot, Aikagi the Animation, it is still an adequate peer to that hentai and I will cover why later on. Much like Aikagi, it gives us a gratifying note to end our session on and you will never be disappointed watching this hentai.


Story : 7

Truth be told, the plot of Aibeya is nothing special but the charm comes from it’s self-awareness of the genre it chooses to occupy. Straight off the bat, in a chibi-styled introduction evoking a comical tone we are brought up to speed with the situation MC-kun finds himself in, returning from home his parents decide to renovate their whole house on a whim and have left him alone on a trip. Conveniently for MC-kun his next door neighbour and childhood friend are going to be accommodating him and inevitably they end up entangled in each others’ lives and bodies. Aibeya like Aikagi wastes no time getting to the confession scene and instead of having our character tiptoe around the elephant in the room, we instantly get to see this relationship blossom which is an absolute necessity for a one episode hentai. Combined with the pace of the show, the ending also helps to push this score over to a 7 which was extremely satisfying (Hint : MC-kun interrupts Aki’s confession).

Art and Animation : 7.5

The art and animation in this show are definitely above average as it is a show built upon both subtle and explosive emotions which the facial expressions from the artwork manage to convey to the audience quite well and enables the viewer to subconsciously develop a greater understanding of the character’s personality as well as sneakily gets us emotionally invested in the relationship. While the animation shows quite a bit of motion recycling, this is made up by the wide array of positions and settings our characters find themselves in, especially the fan-servicey cosplay parts. As always, a more modest approach to character design is appreciated with our main girl not having absolute units of milkers attached to her that would cause scoliosis and cripple her for life.

Characters : 6

While the pacing of character and relationship development had many parallels to Aikagi, one crucial factor missing from this show was the trigger for rapid acceleration. What I mean by that is that there was a greater emotional buildup in the Christmas scene of Aikagi which led to the eventual explosion of emotions however that seems absent from Aibeya which makes the confession much less impactful on me personally. Furthermore there wasn’t much of couple development afterwards, we see them exploring however that felt more like ‘horny teenagers doing horny teenagers things’ while there was an elevation to how the characters in Aikagi developed so although not a letdown, the characters in Aibeya were much more mediocre lacking that extra level of depth.

Wholesome Level : 8

What can I say? I’m a sucker for sweet handholding, love confessing, eyes locked missionary sex. Those two characters living together, in an environment where they are actively encouraged to love each other just melts my virgin, girlfriendless, loner, loser heart more than any young adult film starring two ridiculously good-looking actors stricken with terminal illnesses could. If you’re tired of sitting in the shadows watching an ugly bastard troll rape the shit out of cute girls in a cave, I invite you to walk out of that cave and into the light of vanilla hentai.


Enjoyment : 8

While technically not as impressive or well-written as Aikagi the Animation, Aibeya is another voice resisting the onslaught of horrifying hentai that I willingly choose to consume, wrecking my psyche in the process. It still has a wholesome ending and wholesome emotions all throughout which is all you can ever ask for when browsing random hentai. It doesn’t reek of cashgrab or rushed production and it helped me forget about the overbearing loneliness which continues to strain my heart. If you’re looking for wholesome I wholeheartedly recommend Aibeya the Animation as another guaranteed heartwarmer or at least you won’t feel an onslaught of postnut depression wash over you which is always healthy.

Aibeya The Animation is an OVA by Pink Pineapple. It's a fairly standard vanilla hentai, but I still found it quite enjoyable.

Lets start with the story. To be honest, there isn't a whole lot to it. Basically, a guy is staying at his childhood friend's house because his own home is being renovated. While they're living together, they both start to develop feelings for each other. Most of this happens at the beginning, so the rest of the OVA is mainly just them being lovey-dovey with one another. It's pretty cute.

The art and animation in this OVA was surprisingly decent. I've seen a lot of vanilla hentai that skip out on using more art assets, and rely on limited animation. So needless to say I was worried. Thankfully, both the art and animation turned out to be satisfactory. The voice actors also do a decent job.

There're two main characters in this hentai, Eita and Asuka. Eita is the main male lead, and Asuka is the main female lead. They both have pretty stale personalities. Asuka is somewhat perverted (though only slightly more than Eita), but apart from that, they don't have a lot going for them. They're cute and likeable though, which I guess is all that matters for this story. There's also Asuka's mom, who provides some brief comic relief. I personally found her to be mildly amusing.

Overall, Aibeya is a very simple OVA. It's just plain vanilla, but it still has enough effort put into it to spice things up and keep it from becoming boring. 6.5/10

Would I reccomend you watch this OVA? Yes! Yes I would. It's a very feel-good type anime. So despite its simplicity, you should still come away from it with something.

Sometimes fate brings us together, and sometimes it is just our friends and family giving us that extra push.

Story: Two neighbor's, Kouta and Aki, will be living together after Kouta's family decided to renovate their entire house and agreeing with Aki's parents that he will live with them for the time being. Being childhood friends, it is a bit difficult at first to handle being together in the same house, but as time passes, as well as Aki's mom and Kouta and Aki's best friends respectively hinting at them that they should be together, they finally become a couple and engage in many enjoyable acts together.

While a bit questionable, the story does present itself in a very realistic way and is quite nice. It is the usual situation when something changes between two friend's lives that causes the people around them to start giving the hints of "making the move" and finally becoming something more than just friends. While the pacing of the story does make them a couple rather quickly and make the rest of the episode just different sexual acts they perform, it was still nice. Then again, vanilla hentai often have the simplest plot with no major conflicts to arise from at times. And low key, the mother and friends played a nice part in trying to inch those two to finally take a step together. 3/5

Music: Sadly, the music isn't all that well. Certain sound effects I have heard in other hentai, especially during the sexual acts, and the music that was different didn't stand out all that much. Not that it didn't add to scene properly at times, but it seemed a bit copy paste in terms of music played. 1.2/5

Animation: The environments were quite simple sticking to indoors mainly with very few scenes involving the outdoors and the few that did involve the outdoors, such as the bowling date, did seem a bit iffy, but they turned out good. The scenes and the characters were drawn quite nicely, especially during the cosplay and blowjob scenes, and hardly had much repetition with all the changing sex positions, which was a nice change. Also a nice change is not having the main heroine's chest be so large, C cups at most, so that was a nice sight. All in all, the animation was a bit off for environments, but pulled it off nicely for the characters and scenes. 3.8/5

True to genre: When you see the vanilla tag, you often expect a romance story, and that was delivered. The other tags also played in nicely to the story with a hint of other sexual tags to add, but the primary ones were followed nicely and drawn well. 5/5

Eroticism: While I admit that the sexual scenes were very erotic and did arouse me a bit, vanilla hentai isn't one I often watch for arousal, but instead for the story itself. People often watch vanilla to envision being in a relationship, which I admit I do so when watching vanilla also, but for me, it puts me off to the idea of pleasuring myself while watching something that seems quite sweet to me. Not to say that it didn't spark any arousal in me, but vanilla hentai is one I will always purely enjoy for the story, even though this one was the closest I ever came to enjoying myself while watching it. 3.6/5

Concluding my review: A vanilla hentai is a sweet one, and this one is no exception. While it lacks some creative music and can be a bit off in the animation for its locations, the sweetness and eroticism is nicely expressed through its animation. But it's also nice reminder how sometimes our family and friends can be good wing-mans to help start something that may be one of our biggest achievements.

SUMMARY
Childhood friends become lovers in a very vanilla hentai adventure.

STORY
The story follows the relationship between childhood friends Kouta and Aki. They are both written realistically lacking the usual stereotypical shyness tropes that most OAVs will carry.

Kouta is encouraged by his friends to make a move on Aki who is considered the hottest girl in school. However Kouta plays it cool, but in a matter of minutes is hooking up with Aki. The storyline does not really build much from that.

I would have liked the story to have a bit more build, but with only one episode to work with everything felt rather rushed. I was also not a fan of the hints given by the mother and think a more straight laced mother would have worked well.

VISUALS and EROTICS
Everything looks pretty decent with nice colours and brightness. Without being overblown Aki is given a very sexual shape with large breasts and a nice ass.

The episode features three key sex scenes which really feels like a box ticking effort of eroticism, everything imaginable is covered without going anywhere too extreme. It is all in all a good experience and vanilla hentai experience.

HIGHLIGHT MOMENT
I rather enjoyed the fancy dress photo scene in the final third of the show.

CONCLUSION
A good vanilla experience and recommended for anyone with a vague taste in hentai without being able to reach the highlights required for anyone with a more discerning taste.
