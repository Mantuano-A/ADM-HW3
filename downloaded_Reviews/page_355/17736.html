Score - 8/4

Viewer beware : Futanari included

There seems to be a trend in hentai where it feels like in the middle of production they either chose to give up or forced out a new episode without any source material left. I can’t exactly tell what attributes to this all too common occurrence but it can take a great hentai and leave me with a bitter aftertaste. Koakuma Kanojo falls victim to this trend as we have one of the greatest first episodes I’ve seen in some time with great characters, great animation, funny dialogue and while the premise of “My Succubus Girlfriend Can’t be this Cute” has been played out many times it still is refreshing to see it executed in a way that elevates it past fap material. Yet the second episode pales in comparison to the first and while it starts off strong you slowly begin to realize that they prioritize the fucking over anything else which cheapens the whole experience. Since the first episode consists of two short stories that do not tie into the second episode, I recommend anyone considering watching Koakuma Kanojo stick to the first episode as it would be a waste of bandwidth to download/stream the second episode.

Story - 7/5
The first episode of Koakuma Kanojo, as previously stated, consists of two stories - girl becomes succubus and fucks crush, girl grows a dick and fucks her sister. Both aren’t too wild however they are a bit wilder than the norm and are both themed around the “cursed DVD” trope however they don’t take themselves too seriously and just have fun with the concept i.e. the girl’s sister watching the DVD so she can revenge-fuck her sister. They are sweet, short and simple stories that quickly establish the plot, do some fuckin and leave room for gags in the lulls. Although the jokes themselves aren’t laugh out loud funny, they are still quite amusing and sets a much more chilled mood to the whole experience you know it won’t be some hardcore rape/humiliation/torture shit so you can just relax.
The difference between this and the second episode is that the second episode, while starting out with poking fun at the osananajimi trope, just gets really sexual real quick(yes I am aware I’m watching a hentai) so while it may be ideal for a quick fap, it let me rather underwhelmed as a follow up to the first episode. While both episodes have fairly simple plots, the first episode played around with the concept much more which made it more interesting to watch and since each plot was allotted 10 minutes, it felt like the perfect amount of time before the novelty of the premise ran out. In the case of the second episode, taking almost 30 minutes for a half-baked concept that gets dropped almost immediately turns it into one of the most generic hentai I’ve watched in a while so I quickly got bored. Now comparing the two, it felt like the writers had better grips on the concept in the first episode and it felt like their creative taps dried out by the second episode.

Art and Animation - 8.5/5
My biggest gripe with the hentai was actually the sheer drop in quality between episodes which absolutely baffled me considering they were both of the same series. The first episode, while not the best looking, had a style that suited its light-hearted and comedic attitude complimenting the jokes and dialogue very well. This style was also much more distinct and left an impression on me when I watched it, with dynamic animations, it never idled on a frame for too long. In stark comparison, the second episode looked like your bargain bin hentai that you’d pick up if you had a couple extra cents after buying that new hentai game you’d been saving up for. Visually it was extremely uninteresting and added to my growing boredom for the episode as I continued watching it, acting as a sort of red flag that this episode would definitely not be up to par with the first episode. I just felt genuine disappointment after that first episode had sparked an enthusiasm in hentai I haven’t felt for too long, the second episode comes around to stomp out that flickering flame.

Characters : 7/4
As for the characters, they really are just one-offs so there isn’t a need for them to be engaging as we won’t be seeing some rapid character development in 10 minutes. For the first episode, they had such limited time with each character but they were wise enough to just get straight into things while letting the audience get to know the character contextually through the events and character interactions rather than giving us one-on-one time with the character to start. This approach gives you a sort of “thrown right into things” feeling which can be refreshing compared to shows that take a slower start to things and can appeal to the fapper on the go. While little development is given to these characters, they are loveable from the get go meaning by the time your journey has ended with them, it's just all smiles around. Meanwhile, in the second episode, they are given much more time albeit with a three character ensemble, one over the pair presented in the stories of episode one, however these characters just come off as uninspired, cookiecutter characters that just fall flat after what I had previously been treated to. Coupled with the worse animation quality and art direction, the standard hentai characters just added to the unpleasantness of the whole experience and I was ready to turn it off the moment the credits started rolling

Engagement - 9/3
Engagement seems like the most apt way to differentiate the two episodes because with one I was completely engrossed in the experience while I began to feel very indifferent a quarter of the way into the second episode. Characters stood out more, the art style felt unique and vibrant and the hentai seemed to have a voice of its own during the first episode which excited me after a long break from hentai however the second episode showed me what hentai usually is like which was to my greatest disappointment.

Enjoyment - 8/3
Being as objective as I can be, the second episode isn’t bad compared to the sea of hentai but it just isn’t good either so I wouldn’t bother with it. If you’re looking for something quick and fun that won’t leave you wondering what the hell you just fapped to then just watch the first episode and be done with the series. It really is a good time and personally futa is an added bonus to the whole experience and with the addition of a little incest sprinkled on top how could I resist? The second episode felt like porridge that lacked any seasoning while the first was a juicy steak with an exotic yet familiar spice rub that surprises you while never becoming tedious to eat.
