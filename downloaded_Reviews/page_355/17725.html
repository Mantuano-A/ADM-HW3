The sound, of course, is fairly nice throughout, for what that is worth in a Hentai title. This usually means appealing moans from the girl. This bill is fulfilled.

If you are looking for fodder for your sexual impulses, the first and second episodes serve very well. As for the third and fourth, they do too, but actually have a storyline, so they can be watched like a regular anime. Okay, that's false, but at least it adds extra entertainment.

The final thing to be mentioned is the comedy. Mizugi Kanojo is a very goofy Hentai, providing a good amount of comedy for those who prefer the non-serious stuff.

Overall, Mizugi Kanojo is an excellent Hentai title, and anybody who watches Hentai really should give Mizugi Kanojo a viewing.
