Writing a Review for Upotte!! OVA
========================

First thing to know about this OVA episode like most OVA's from animes with a large girl cast, Expect the fanservice.

There is minimal to no story line in this, It is basically a fun beach side episode, only difference they are camping riverside.

The art is well done like its ONA counterpart, The sounds are up to standard, The character's look a little older than they did from the main season,

If you are looking for a little fanservice after watching the main season here it is.

A poor excuse for an OVA whose only purpose was to show breasts. I love the characters of this show and its unique premise, but what really carried the series was the plot. This OVA has almost no plot.

That's not to say that it's horrible. It's OK, keeping the characters we have come to love through the show's first season, but it doesn't add anything to them, and doesn't even focus on the fact that they're assault rifles.

That's basically all I have here, but MAL wants a longer review, so I'll keep rambling on. It's actually been a long time since I've seen this OVA so I'm writing much of this from memory, so I apologize if any information is incorrect. The story is really what kills it here.

I'm not one to hate on fan service, that's why my score is as high as it is, because overall the only thing this has going for it is fanservice.
