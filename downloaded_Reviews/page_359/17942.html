Got a fetish like cosplay sex, dominating partners, or group orgies? Looking for people of the opposite sex who also have that fetish? Well that's what Namanaka Hyaku Percent is about.

Recently a new trend has been going around. People are wearing or carrying different colored shoelaces (called "shinpa") around. Each color denotes a different fetish. Our protagonist happens to have one for cosplay sex. He works at a convenience store, and notices a girl who often shops there has the same colored shinpa.

One day, he finally decides to make his move, but she happens to notice his shinpa first and is eager to try it out with him. They go back to his apartment and enjoy their fetishes with his large array of costumes. This hentai is that it's kind of a strange harem. I can never exactly tell whether the initial girl with the cosplay fetish is intending to put the main guy into the situations he ends up involved in.

After temporarily parting ways, the girl left a new, different colored shinpa with the guy. The busty manager of the apartment complex where the main character is staying notices this shinpa and takes him back to her home. He soon finds out from her that this shinpa means that the person enjoys being dominated, and he is "forced" into having sex in an elevator (which seems to never reach it's destination for some reason).

The second episode is all lolis. First it's a young girl with probably the most revealing karate outfit ever who has a fetish for anal sex. Then it's a girl who is revealed to actually be the creator of the shinpa as well as the first girl's younger sister who enjoys group sex. The second one carries over into the third episode (with my favorite sex scene in the beginning) and then the first girl comes back for a three-way with the main character and her sister.

This hentai has a strange art style in many ways. The characters all have huge, colorful eyes with irises that take up 100% of their eye. It's not necessarily bad, but it does take some time to get used to.

This is a good hentai, but personally I did not really like the second episode. The anal loli scene didn't really appeal much to me, but that's just a personal thing and not really a problem at all if you like that. The rest of the second episode after that was mostly plot with a little teasing and wasn't really very good. However, the third episode was fully entertaining and the first sex scene in the first episode was also very good.

Overall, this is a slightly above average hentai and if you enjoy fetishes at all, then I recommend it. The art takes a little getting used to, but the first sex scene is good enough to compensate.
