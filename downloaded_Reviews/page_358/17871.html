Yes, back to reviewing porn!
A friend recommended this to me with just the premise "you'll love it, Crusty". I was wondering what it was about this series that had made my friend so eager to tell me to watch it. And I saw the light, and saw that it was good.

It's great to see a lovely vanilla series come along, seeing as hentai as of late has been teetering towards the hardcore side (I'm pretty sure that Euphoria will go on forever). Now, I may be a girl, but that doesn't mean that I don't like the rough stuff. I happen to like S and M, things like that. It's nice to get a breath of fresh air every now and again, and Amakano is just what the doctor ordered. Most likely Doctor Shameless, but that's a hentai for another time. Its hard to tell cause the title was in katakana, but I'm guessing that the title is a mix of the words 甘い/amai and 彼女/kanojo meaning sweet girlfriend. I will be so embarrassed if I'm just totally wrong. And that's exactly what this series is- very sweet.

The art, oh dear lord, the art. Hentai NEEDS to keep going in this direction, style-wise, just more polished, more professional-looking, and preferably less of that "Queen Bee" bollocks that seems to be seeping in through the cracks lately. Hentai seems to be getting colour schemes, and it's becoming more and more detailed. Basically, good all-round. One specific thing I must mention about the art in this hentai is the backgrounds. The snowy cabin, the inside, just everything. They are nothing less than beautiful and I dare to think of how long it must have taken to paint them. The whole hentai is gorgeous. Even the detail of the character designs impressed me.

The voice acting is notable since the female lead manages to stay believable and not become annoying with her loudness during the sexy times. Her trait of shyness stays prominent even through the sex scenes, something pretty rare. I'm not talking about a tsundere's shyness, I mean genuine shyness in a more adorable way. A bad habit hentai has is introducing a shy girl and then the second things become sexual, her personality goes out the window and she becomes just as loud and over-the-top as other more extroverted girls in the series. It's also nice to hear a younger boy protagonist, someone who is more of the "boku" type rather than "ore". I also like that he's not completely silent during sex like other protags tend to be.

Both character designs are attractive, but obviously the girl is more detailed than the boy. The main trait for both of them is shyness, since they both seem to stutter a lot and are very respectful of each other. It was a nice change to see a truly loving couple in a hentai, and it has somewhat reaffirmed my faith in the genre. I don't know where else I'm going to mention this, so I might as well do it now. The female character has odd-coloured eyes. I loved seeing something different, since I end up watching a lot of same-y things. I found the odd colours strangely attractive. Her cat-like eye shape is also worth noting, since a lot of shows tend to opt for more loli-esque, round eyes. She's not just a sexy female character; she's a genuinely beautiful girl with an adorable personality.

Obviously, I liked this series. It is outstanding, but didn't exactly blow me away enough to get a 10. It does however get a 9 for breaking away from the harem bullshit we're all expected to gulp down lately. The sex has a nice build-up; it doesn't just happen out of nowhere, and I find this to be a massive plus. the only thing that wasn't worth praise is that it could be considered "boring", since the episode I have seen is just "Here is a couple. This is their story. This is their sex. Cute, right? The end." I definitely don't think this way, but I know some people might not want something quite so vanilla.

Keep it up, hentai industry. Keep. It. Up. I'm liking it.

First review, big whoop. Kinda spoiler free.

Amakano is probably a portmanteau of Sweet and Girl/Girlfriend. It's a vanilla series based off a homonymous visual novel (that I haven't read), and depicts part of what I assume to be the playroutes of all three girls. There's one girl for each of the first three episodes, and then shorter scenes with all three in the additional episode to finish the four.

Believable settings or storylines are almost unrelated to enjoyment for me in a hentai. Especially relevant in an anime based off a visual novel, where sexual scenes usually represent considerable progress along a relationship route. I wasn't put off by anything story-wise so that gets a solid 5.

My completely biased opinion is that the art was fine. Each girl had decent aesthetic appeal, distinguishing facial features and it was easy to notice changes in expression from scene to scene. I didn't notice any glaring errors, the detail on their hairstyles was pretty good, and they looked great during sex. Even got two three angles per act which was nice (and a decent range of positions). I didn't feel the colour palette was dull, but the lighting could have been better. Overall a good 7.

The voices fit the characters and ranges of emotion and excitement were well-conveyed. There was plenty of dialogue exchanged before, during and after the scenes so it didn't suffer from any major awkwardness. The lines were stock, but the sound effects were decent, and the protagonist stayed fairly quiet when sessions got more intense. This will take a bit of a knock though, because I disliked the voice/performance over the strawberry-blonde which unfortunately offended my ears and 'viewing experience'. I think another 6 is fair.

Wasn't keen on the main character, but it's not particularly important. The girls were likeable enough, but unmemorable. I was satisfied that each girl's portrayed in a light that brings adorableness to the fore/ suggests greatest devotion to the protagonist. I won't remember any of their names (days from now) or anything unique about their personalities, so I won't go above a 7.

It was a mellow, pleasant watch. Didn't quite have me scrambling for a box of tissues, but I would recommend it to someone who liked vanilla hentai.

I wonder if romance could ever be considered a tag in hentai.

Story: For the first three episodes, each one focuses on an individual girl at a sort of winter lodge/mansion and the last episode has each girl have their own sex scene. From warming up during a winter storm to coming home for Valentines Day to visiting your home town after so long, each girl presents a different scenario for an intimate moment.

The story for each girl is quite lovely, with my personal favorite being Mizuki from the first episode. The idea of being inside a nice, cozy home during a winter storm is by far one of the most romantic scenarios a couple could have together. It seems a bit repetitive that each girl is placed in similar situations for a winter lodge, but the different scenarios that take place far make up for the repetitive nature. The only issue I have would be that the main male character is the same one for each girl, making this seem a bit off putting. In conclusion, this seems like such a sweet story to watch, especially if you are warming up on a cold night or even, dare I say, with your partner. 3.5/5

Music: there isn't much music used, even atmospheric, which I actually find to be more appropriate for the story and become more appealing. Music if often used in hentai to make it more erotic and arousing for the viewer, but no music added at certain points made it a bit more enjoyable due to its sense of realism, as ironic as that is. The other great thing about this hentai is the ending song, which is one of my favorite songs I have heard and learned. Certain scenes without music probably could've been better with music, but they are still good without it. 3.8/5

Animation: I wouldn't say the animation was bad, but it possibly could've been a bit better. The design and shading used for characters from lighting to the environments to the sex scenes were quite enjoyable, and there were hardly, if any, repetitive scenes used. In fact, the animation seemed to fit the hentai very well, but I'm certain it could've been even better. 4/5

True to genre: Vanilla is by far the most defined way to describe this hentai. As for the other genres, it is a bit questionable. Being four episodes, some of these genres were limited to certain girls, such as virgin for episode 3 and bondage in episode 4 for Mizuki only. Being different genres for each girl does add variety as each girl is different in kinks and figure, so despite there being a few genres limited to only certain girls, I feel that was best. 3.7/5

Eroticism: I found no arousal when watching this hentai, but that doesn't take away from how erotic it is for a couple. Being together to keep each other warm, celebrating your first Valentine's Day together, or seeing each other after so long, these moments are so precious and can be so intimate as memories. This did spark some personal fantasies I would love to have should I ever have a partner. I would say it definitely has a nice sense of arousal, not of being erotic, but of the passion that come from a couple together. 5/5

Concluding my review: Amakano is, personally for me, one of my favorite vanilla hentai to watch, because of its reminder to me of how I long for the moments I can share with a partner of my own. Despite it's flaws and not being a perfect score in a logical sense, this hentai will stand as perfect for me in its emotional sense.
