Energy Kyouka!! Excels in both animation, sound and plot. You know a hentai is good when you're not even horny and you still watch it, I've done this many times with this series. There's something about the bathroom scene in the first episode which makes me feel excited and want to fuck my own computer. This hentai helped me change my mind about EDGE (The company responsible for the animation), I felt as if 2016 was a good year for them releasing very hot and sexy masterpiece titles such as "Energy Kyouka!!" and "Dropout". Overall this is one of EDGE's magnum opus's and it will be very difficult task for them to out do this and Dropout. I give it an overall 8/10 and is a good starter hentai for those new into the hentai game as it isn't too fucked up in anyway. Personally one of my Top 20 hentai.
