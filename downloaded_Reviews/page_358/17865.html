There is a lot you can find in the incest genre of Hentai, and by a lot I do mean a lot ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°). The incest genre has a pretty good reputation with me when it comes to quality. I feel Hentai's biggest weakness pinpointed down to a specific genre would have to be Rape and NTR, not because of what is stands for (Because this is a peace of fiction and is not meant to be taken literal... which I hope everyone understands) but because of how god awful the shit is in this genre. The incest genre on the other hand is one of the most consistent genres when it comes to quality. The characters are usually much better in this when compared to NTR because the female cast is not brought down in personality by some fat ugly fuck. And I just don't know why... but when it comes to art and camera direction this genre never seems to fail. Long intro aside today we are talking about a hentai which is apart of the incest genre and is one of the best in this already amazing genre. I am talking about none other than "Ane Koi: Suki Kirai Daisuki"!

Story: 7/10.
While the incest genre excels in characters, the same cannot be said about the story. Ane Koi's premise is borderline average incest story, but I will give it credit where it is due, it is not a bad premise, just nothing groundbreakingly new. The pacing for these types of hentai's great! It has an episodic feel to it while at the same time I feel like progression is actually happening in the relationship between the Hayami siblings.

Art: 9/10.
Now some might disagree with my rating for this but I do have my reasons. The art definitely does look a bit outdated considering this is a 2013 hentai but for some reason it blends with the tone of the type of story it is fairly well. And really the old art complaint can be easily removed by just watching it in 1080p. The Animation is very nice however. It is fluid when it needs to be but choppy when it needs to be as well. And finally, easily the best part of this segment, the camera angles. Although it's art is nothing to be praised about the animation studio works with what they have and make sure to put their hentai in the right angles for each scene to immerse the audience despite the debatable animation.

Sound: 8/10.
The voice actors are very good which can't be denied. Saya's VA really gets into her role and enhances the hentai. The only major fault is some syncing issues. Sometimes the voices just don't match the character's mouth flaps and it does get a bit annoying at times.

Character: 10/10.
This should really be no surprise here. The real reason why character is so high for these type of shows is because almost all Incest Hentai's try to develop the relationships. In the case of the Hayami siblings, the evolution of their relationship from start to finish in these short 2 episodes is crazy. And that is not all, both end up developing as individuals too. Saya is one of my favorite hentai female leads of all time! Not only is her design super appealing all around but her development throughout is what makes her such an engrossing character for me!

Enjoyment: 10/10.
I enjoyed all of Ane Koi. It was a great ride that made use of it's budget very well. At the same time, it also had a phenomenal duo that truly developed by the end of the series. Sadly the character of Saya was little more than a single chapter character in the manga, so there will probably never be a squeal :(

Overall: 9/10. There were some parts Ane Koi struggles with but there is no denying that the characters in this show are one of a kind and some of the best hentai has to offer! YOU REALLYYYYYY NEEEDDDDD TO WATCH THIS!

Summary : This anime is about a boy who quietly obsesses over a lust for his elder sister and eventually decides to act on his desires much to his sister's initial reluctance and annoyance. Eventually she also begins to love him back and the two end happily together.

Story : A school boy finds himself infatuated by his busty elder sister and with a little accidental prompting from his friend at school decides to have sex with her even if she refuses.

The first sex scene certainly can be considered non-consensual and a little unsettling to watch, but slowly both characters begin to bond with one another and love each other both physically and emotionally.

The four sex scenes make up a huge amount of the two episodes with most the story being driven during these scenes. There are some parts where the story seems to suddenly advance to a new situation without no explanation, for example the boy ends up in hospital without any prior warning or suggestion. At times the story will also resolve conflicts within seconds of them being introduced and leaves the show feeling a little badly paced.

Characters : The characters are both well written and have a reasonable level of realism, the girl is designed to be attractive and is given a very large pair of breasts.

Animation / design : I thought the animation style of this show was great, everything looked very crisp and fresh. The characters also looked brilliant.

Overall I think the show was pretty good, it looked great and had a decent amount of well put together sex scenes. The incest angle was played up quiet a lot, so if that is something you are interested in. I would definitely recommend it.
