But let's get straight to the point here:

The first episode is really the cream of the entire series, the rest is fairly bad. In short the MC is in love with a certain waitress, fantasizing about her all the time and planning on capturing her heart. Unbeknown to the MC, the girl is a fiery nympho and his friend casually plows her behind his back (literally) while he sits there sipping coffee oblivious to the spectacle going on just a few feet away from him. Fortunately, MCs friend notices his amorous feelings and decides to help his sheepish counterpart in his own little way that may or may not involve hiring a slut. Will he succeed?

Overall, not bad but not good either when episodes 3 and 4 are taken into consideration. 1 and 2 were still enjoyable within some moral framework. The waitress is not actually anyone's girlfriend, all sex is consensual etc.

Episodes 3 and 4 are downright mental though. Do not watch those... unless you enjoy that kind of thing I guess?

I've watched few other hentai series to make an analysis by comparison, so I'll take this one solely by itself.


STORY: 6
The main story is focused on the first episode, which features four characters: a waitress, the boy she likes and who also likes her, a perverted man and his perverted female friend. The two adults are responsible for molesting the poor youngsters and forcing them to have sex.

Episodes 2, 3 and 4 don't have a consistent, linear plot. Instead, each of them is composed of two different stories: one related to the main plot (or at least containing characters from the first episode) and the other with a completely new story and characters. In my opinion these unconnected, singular stories (particularly the last one) were far more interesting than the main plot.

The parts of the episodes 3 and 4 that show the main plot seem like mere fillers and there is nothing interesting about them besides sex scenes.

Considering these original unrelated stories inside the episodes, which are in my opinion quite interesting, I can't give less than 6 of story overall. If it was all about the rest, I would surely give 5 or less.

ART: 7
There is one big problem with art in this series: Tomoe's hair colour and hairstyle magically change through episodes, and this seems a bit confusing. Despite this, the characters' physical design in general and the environment are quite good, so there is no other concerns about art here. I'll take good.

SOUND: 8
The sound is fine. I particularly loved the last outro music. I'll give an 8.

CHARACTER: 6
Tomoe, the short-haired woman in red glasses, is the only character who appears in all four episodes and her intentions swap different times between good and evil. She both sexually abuses the waitress, Tamaki and demonstrates strong compassion and love by the two boys she liked. This drastic contrast is quite weird, I must admit.

Tamaki and Ohashi are tipically shy, weak, defenseless teenagers and barely hesitate in doing what they are forced to.

Some characters in the three short stories look a bit artificial, but I think that this artificiality is what makes them very special, too.

None of them are complex at all. I'll take fair.

ENJOYMENT: 6
Sex scenes are very intense and may reflect both a strong love relationship between the two envolved and a sadistic sarcasm of one by another (considering the feelings and needs of the other). If you like mild, cute hentai this is not for you.

Sometimes this anime did inflict the feelings of mystery and fear to me, meaning that some stories can affect emotions, while others are rather boring as I said.

OVERALL: FAIR.

Accelerando is unusual as each episode explores different short stories with different characters. This show could've been better since some of these characters had potential to be memorable like in a School Days kind of way but nothing great comes out of it other than a couple of good but samey h-scenes.

Episode 1 introduces Tamaki and Ohashi, they secretly like each other but have trouble expressing their love because they're both your typical timid anime characters. They get the most screen time and are the most boring and predictable characters. Girl gets blackmailed, has to do sexual favors, virgin boyfriend or love interest finds out, ends up not minding at all and goes along with the flow. It's bad enough to have equally shy protagonists but also with the same old hentai tropes makes it really hard to watch and it goes on like this for the rest of the show with these two, which is why they're the absolute worst characters this show has to offer.

The first half of episode 2 gets better, it's a backstory of a character of episode 1. Pretty good though with the same blackmailing trope, then there are different characters introduced that are just that much into sex that they can't control themselves whatsoever, it's basically 1% story and 99% hentai after that. My short summary would be

Episode 1 is terrible.
Episode 2 is better but only the first half.
Episode 3 terrible first half, good second half because "step-bro" is kinda funny imo.
Episode 4 starts off creepy and good but immediately falls into cringe territory and the second half sucks.

The art is acceptable and animation are okay, unfortunately I haven't seen an uncensored version which is really distracting for some of the good h-scenes. The sound is also okay I guess the wet spaghetti sounds during penetration gets redundant and the voice acting gets really annoying at times but it's a hentai.
