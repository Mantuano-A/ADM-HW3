And the sound... well... you hear it... was there a soundtrack? I didn't really recognize. And the dubbing is just normal... good... what else can i say? =D

Score: 5/10

CHARACTER
Yeyeye, again no deepness, no background just "Big things poppin'" (or however the newschool HipHop Fans may call it:) The characters are designed like in "Milk Junkies" (by the way, i actually thought that Bakunyuu Oyako was Milk Junkies AAH! But it's obvious what the producers try to provoke with this Hentai, so for that i can carress them over they'r heads and say "You did your best!"

Score: 4/10

OVERALL
The only thing in Bakunyuu Oyako what is really awesome are the comedy parts, when the main character is talking to hisself, or when he confesses, that he is a "Breast loving alien from outta space". Erm; to find this funny, you've got to watch it, so u understand what i mean.
But story, art and sound is nothing special and there is no big... commercial break for some bad jokes... "change" or anything like it. And to make it worse, in some scenes (as i mentioned above) they looped 2 animations for like 1 or 2 minutes (i'm not kidding.) And this is really annoying. I mean come on, Bakunyuu Oyako only got 2 episodes, give us everything! And i mean everything! ;)
Overall Bakunyuu Oyako is just "standart" normally i would give it (for the comedy parts) 6 points, but the scenes and the not-so-good animation and art quality is dragging it down, so:

Score: 5/10. Nothing u have to see, but if u see it, u may enjoy it at least a bit.
