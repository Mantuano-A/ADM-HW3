Well, for a moment there I thought the OVA will be just another fan service.

Turns out the OVA of Witch Craft Works itself, in my opinion, generate more excitement for me than the actual series itself. It is very light-hearted and to be frank, the comedy is very refreshing.

Bringing the bro-con topic of the main character's sister and WWE-ish featuring bear and penguin, the OVA is just an extension to the main series.

The art and the sound are very much the same with the usual series. The character however, stole the spotlight in this OVA.

I have to say that the OVA is much more enjoyable and comedic compared to the main series.

For overall resultt, this is one OVA that is good, if not, better than the main series in term of enjoyment.

Witch Craft Works: Takamiya-kun to Imouto no Warudakumi (2015)

STORY:

OVA about Kasumi gaining an alliance with Tanpopo to take down the Princess. They recruit other members of Tower Witches to achieve this goal. Ultimately it is based around Kasumi and her Bro-Con ways that is seen by all but her. They proceed with their plan and the expected results. The actual episode picks up directly from the end of the 12th episode. It also contains a funny fight scene with the cute mentor Rinon.

ART:

The art is the same as before just perhaps a little less polished off so the quality is slightly worse, but it is far from bad however.

SOUND:

Same as the main series. All very good and to standard with the great ending song.

CHARACTER:

Get to know more about the other supporting Tower Witches and more about Kasumi which is really nice to see.

ENJOYMENT:

The episode is very enjoyable and brings some extra hilarity to the already great series.

OVERALL:

Absolutely worth a watch if you enjoyed the series.
