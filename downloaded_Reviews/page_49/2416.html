The strange thing about the "Onegai Teacher" series is that, for an anime with a premises that's so obviously based on fetish fantasy straight out of someone's head, it had zero hardcore fanservice (as in panty shots, nudity etc). I'm sure that a lot of people who were attracted to the series based on the synopsis were hoping to see a little more than a hot teacher pouting and making a few cute poses.

Well, this OVA special goes about rectifying that problem, letting loose all those elements that, for some strange reason, were suppressed in the original series. In other words, this OVA is pretty much all about the fanservice, so there isn't really much to talk about. What's really funny though, is that they try to disguise the purpose of this blatent fanservice episode by pretending that it actually contains a meaningful message, a kind of "see - there's actually a real purpose to this episode" claim.

...Yeah, right. I didn't buy it for a second. Please don't try and pretend this OVA is anything other than a cheap excuse for fanservice. Even by "Onegai Teacher" lowly standards, this is a low point (or a high one, depending on your point of view), and THAT is saying something...

I'd have to admit that this wasn't quite as bad as I remember, but it's still not very good at all.

The characters, art, animation, music and voicework are of the same standard as the TV show, so no need to go back over that. Where the show seriously fails is in the story department.

More to the point, the plot in this OVA is ultimately redundant. The 'problem' that Hatsuho sets out to solve doesn't exist because it was resolved in the TV series. Indeed, in the opening minutes it's revealed that Kei and Mizuho have no problems in that department and need no help. It's overall in bad taste, the dialogue is juvenile and not funny and Hatsuho's actions.....yeah, ewww. It's really a pretty poor excuse for softcore porn. The other characters show up pretty much to only say 'oh look, Kei's with some hot chick!'

If the OVA didn't come bundled with the TV series, I'd say give it a miss. It's not funny and there's just no point to it.

While many judge this as simple "fan-service/ecchi" and deem it horrendous, I disagree.


As a whole, Onegai Sensei was humorous, dramatic, well-paced, and great in general. However, the series did come off with some "forced-drama" as well as being maybe a little sad overall to some, but it was a great piece.


That being said, this OVA, as a standalone, is not that great. However, when watched after the series, it bring a refreshing change of pace with our favorite characters. There were a lot more expressions and "lols" moments than the average Onegai Sensei episode.


It was not a horrendous OVA, but a nice change-of-pace epilogue.
Since my biggest aspect of any anime is enjoyment, I give this a 9~9.5/10 overall!

This will be a quick review.

The only reason I touched on this OVA was to see if I could get even more of a closure to the show. I liked episode 12, it finished stuff nicely but I was selfish and wanted another episode since I liked Onegai Teacher so much. To say the least, I was disappointed with the OVA, it really didn't have the same feel as the rest of the series. Though I did watch it all and enjoyed parts here and there, it was really what I was looking for. Overall, it was decent watch, I guess but I wouldn't pair this with the rest of the series.

The only good side to this OVA with me at least was to see what Onegai Teacher could have been at its worst, it is possible that the whole series might have been this untasteful and meaningless like a lot of shows now. This gives me a total new appreciation for Onegai Teacher and for that, I am glad that I saw this OVA.
