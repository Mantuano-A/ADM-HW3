This title here is the genre-defining piece for NTR. NTR for those of you who don't know stands for netorare which means cuckhold, and it's supposed to get a rise out of people (in more ways than one). Stay away from this if you're a vanilla watcher but definitely check this out if you're a fan of the genre or aren't turned off by it.

The best way to describe Triangle Blue or any NTR is like a trainwreck, you know it's awful but at the same time it's so shocking you can't look away. That's the way it is with this title. Enter Sawamura, a salaryman with a low sex drive and looks like Kyon from Haruhi. Happily married to Akane until one day one of her childhood friends show up. From there on it's pretty predictable since the guy's a slob and just freeloads in Kyon's place, helping himself to food and Akane's body.

The animation quality was pretty good back in its time and even holds up today. The sex scenes used good angles to keep the action interesting, and it was hard to get bored, but easy to get hard.
