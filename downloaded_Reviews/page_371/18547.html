I never thought I'd be writing a review on some random porn I watched on a whim, but I hated this one so much that I wanted to save other people from watching this garbage.

Porn, especially hentai, have a huge range of crazy plot ideas -- and this one's plot didn't really bother me at first. It is set in the future where people below 18 are allowed to have sex and they're also allowed to have children and get married. As someone who was below the age of 18 when I watched it was hoping for something romantic since the plot initially seemed to be focused on some young teenagers falling in love and getting married. That seems pretty tame considering some of the really awful plots most porn have.

Well let me tell you -- they had me fooled because it wasn't that tame. Are there more brutal hentai out there? Yes, but most of the time they don't have a romantic first "arc" and they definitely don't have a boyfriend who is in love with his partner and is trying to get them both out of a bad situation. Focusing so much on this element makes you really want them to succeed and get out of poverty.

Story: 1/10
The point of porn is to get turned on -- not make your audience feel disgusted with themselves or be anxious/worried about plot elements distracting from sex scenes. The part of this porn that is terrible is the second "couple" (there are two couples you follow in the story). The first couple loses their engagement ring to end their arc in the story -- and that ring is picked up by some poor teens who live in like a sewer type area, and they are too poor to be able to buy "a lyft" to the surface. The details don't really matter, but the guy can use the ring to get social security information and get some money so that he and this girl can go to the surface. The first part of the porn's plot was sweet and romantic. So, initially, I am thinking this is going to be two people escaping poverty and are going to get a romantic ending. I should have guessed it was going to get depressing when it brought about the plot element that the girlfriend works as a whore for extra money. I thought, oh wow, I guess they're going for a bit more hyper-realistic vibe for a couple in extreme poverty in this dystopian society. Now because of this when her sorta boyfriend leaves to go get the money (from this engagement ring they found/stole) to take them to the surface -- she is at their little home, packing, and then a huge gang of adult men come and continuously rape her. So, her boyfriend is freaking out because they need to get to the surface before they're found out by the police for having stolen money and he doesn't understand why she isn't at the meetup place. Anyway, pretty much everything goes wrong and after the rapists leave, the boyfriend finds her tied up BDSM style and he starts crying as he is cutting her free. I would say the last scene itself is the saddest and disturbing because it appears like she doesn't recognize him and is asking him to have sex with her, and it's just extremely sad. My goal when I watch porn is not to be depressed afterward. Not to mention the focus of this anime, in the second arc, was on an underaged girl being brutally raped by adult men -- which just makes it that much worse! I hated this hentai so much -- it deserves to be reviewed by ShadowACS on his Ticket to Damnation channel.


Art: 3/10
It was just boring average art -- absolutely nothing worth noting.

Sound: 5/10
The music wasn't really that bad. Some was even a little good, but it played during disturbing scenes which just angered me -- so overall I'm still not going to give it a good rating. Besides, good music doesn't save bad hentai.

Characters: 5/10
There was barely any character development because it is hentai, but I did feel sympathetic to the poverty the two teens in the second arc were living in -- and the disturbing scene at the end made the characters more fleshed out and human -- more than a majority of hentai characters -- so for that I gave them a 5.

Enjoyment: 1/10
This hentai was garbage and I hate it.

First things first : It's important to make the distinction between RESPECTING/VALUING something and LIKING/ENJOYING it.

In my opinion, the MANGA "Implicity" is a GOOD manga, I RESPECT and VALUE it.
I do NOT LIKE/ENJOY it.

And that's its whole point. I can think of a few other similar mangas (most notably the "Hime Kankan" series by Tsukusun). The GOAL of this sort of mangas is to make you feel SICK. They are depicting horrifying things with the intent to make the reader feel horrified from them, not entertained, let alone aroused. So if you hate reading them, it means they're probably doing their job well.

=>

The anime adaptation "Implicity" is CRIMINALLY BAD.
Not just because of the mediocre audio or extremely poor art/animation, those are minor details (and unfortunately pretty common in the hentai industry).

The real problem with this adaptation is that it completely misses the point of the manga. The colorful and light-hearted artstyle is completely at odds with the nausea-inducing theme of the story. This adaptation tries to make arousing a manga that aims at being repulsive. You could almost say that this adaptation tries to force the story to become the opposite of what it was designed as.


So yeah, I regret I can't give a lower score than 1.
