I'm writing this review for everyone who clicked on this entry for a nostalgia trip and was disappointed in one way or another. For a short background of the singer, She is an Utaite named Lon (ろん) that covers many songs from other artists or vocaloids.

A lot of people don't understand that this cover isn't a tribute to Jude and Mary's legacy or Rurouni Kenshin rather it was chosen because it referenced animals in the lyrics. Although I can't say that it wasn't chosen for its popularity.

Every song in this album is a popular song one way or another that references animals in the lyrics. Another notable song that she covers in the album is "Blue Bird" by Ikimono-gatari, third opening for Naruto Shippuuden.

I do agree that the jumbled 'wubs' wasn't the best decision in the world however, I did enjoy the singing that's quite on par with her content in general. How some people received it as "nails on a chalkboard", I assume they dislike it because it doesn't fit their niche or did not mesh well with their nostalgia factor but the vocal range of the song in wasn't much different than the original.

Overall, I believe it holds its own and is a great cover. If you liked the song (aside from the 'wub' , feel free to check out her other songs. If you disliked it, thanks for giving it a shot.

After hearing the first opening of Rurouni kenshin (Sobakasu) I was surprised. It is a beautiful song with amazing lyrics and a gorgeous melody, I gave it a 10 on my review of the series and suddenly I saw this is the last entry for this series and well, covers for anime openings or any kind of remix/cover is worse than the original unless it is some nightcore mix and some handpicked covers. It is simply bad and I did not like this song.

The series and opening have to do with samurais and the Japanese revolution, we see some "harsh" scenes and the singing is really passionate. This cover took that song and added some new melodies to it which just downgraded it and made it sound worse. I am gonna give the story a 5 since it did not represent the opening or anime well.

The visuals are pretty repetitive and only change like 3 times, I did not find them to be really bad and I really don't want to call everything bad because Lon just wanted to make a cover of a legendary opening and just ruin it. I would give them a 7.

On to the song, imagine the original but with some new melodies which made it worse and the singer's voice being slightly tweaked. I am giving the sound a 4.

Characters, well we had a girl who just run and did some stuff. This is a music video and she is just a random character we don't know so I am giving it a 7. It looked fine.

Enjoyment, eh I would rate it a 6. Just go listen to the original opening and not this. This does nothing better than the original and its only purpose in life is to let you jam another really short episode into your episode count. 4/10 since it had no reason to exist and it was overall average with a bad representation of the theme and a butchered song which is normally amazing. The visuals weren't amazing and they didn't keep me on the edge of my seat or something but they pass.
