Since no one is writing reviews here yet, I'd gladly be the first one to do so. No worries, I'd be gentle.

First off, the story. It's nothing special. It's cute, it's funny, and it has a nice pacing. 6/10

Now characters. I think they really did a great job of coming up with original dialogues for them that doesn't contradicts with their canon personalities. Great stuff and clever character interactions. 7/10

Art and Sound both get an 8/10. It's explanatory since it's ufotable. The reason why it ain't 10/10 is because the movements are choppy, looks like 4 frames per second. But the art is gorgeous as usual. As well as there's no off models. The sound is just the usual too.

Lastly, my enjoyment. I enjoyed this one because it is short and hilarious. Like, less than 5 minutes per episode. It's not Gintama level of hilariousness, but it was still pretty good. 7/10

Overall a 7.2/10
Good stuff. And won't certainly waste your time since it's short as hell. And if you're a fan of KnY jokes in general, then you'd enjoy this as well.

Ah, Valentine's Day, the worst day to be single.

Demon Slayer just released this 4 episode special to celebrate Valentine's Day, featuring the main cast of characters in a school setting as they're put through different scenarios.

This special is quite basic and is nothing special, it's just more Demon Slayer for those wanting more of the series. There isn't much to say about the production values as Ufotable probably knocked this out in a couple of weeks of production. The artwork is chibi-like and there isn't much movement or fluidity in the animation.

There were some enjoyable parts, but nothing else impressive. You can easily finish this in 10 minutes or so, so you won't lose anything by watching this.

6/10.

Demon Slayer released this 4 ep. long special to celebrate Valentine’s day. Each episode is roughly two minutes long and is set in the Kimetsu Academy AU, following the disastrous adventures of Zenitsu trying to find a Valentine for this special day.
One thing that was a serious let-down for me was the fact that characters like Shinobu, Kanao and even Nezuko did not get any screen-time at all, regardless of Shinobu being a teacher in the Kimetsu Academy AU and Nezuko being one of the main characters and being one of Zenitsu’s romantic interests.
The animation wasn’t over the top either, it was very basic but the facial expressions were cute and hilarious. The character’s personalities weren’t tweaked for the sake of this special and the interactions between Zenitsu and the other characters like Tanjiro, Giyuu, Rengoku etc. was fun and enjoyable.
I personally would give this a 6.5/10. If you’re into the comedy that Demon Slayer provides, then you might find this enjoyable as well.
