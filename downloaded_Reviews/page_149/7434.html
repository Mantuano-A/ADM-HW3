This recap was actually a really good idea on the companies part. Especially considering that the sequel, Chaos;Child, didn't come out until about 8 years after Chaos;Head. I finished Chaos;Head the weekend prior to the release of Chaos;Child so I would be ready for the new season; I did this with a few shows on my to watch list since they were getting sequels this season as well. Even though I recently watch Chaos;Head, it was still nice to have a quick recap of the major events before getting into the new series. Chaos;Head is kind of a confusing series at times though so this recap may not help everyone remember the important events. I do feel like it lacked some information, specifically a recap on the ending of the series. I'm still giving this an 8/10 because it actually is a really recap to get you thinking about the events that happened in preparation for Chaos;Child. I won't rate this higher than an 8 though because I still feel like it lacked some information as I already said.

Honestly, this recap is better than the entirety of the Chaos;Head anime. This is a good start to the Chaos;Child anime. It helps us to get ready for the anime, which is great since they adapted the important points on the visual novel. If some of the people are wondering why there is things that weren't in the anime because the studio decided to follow the visual novel more than the anime.

One thing that I really liked that they added vn-only scenes, it helps people that didn't watch the prequel before the sequel, although since this recap only gives a quarter of information, you might need to watch/read the prequel.

This recap is okay. I will give it a 6, made me hyped up for episode 1 of Chaos;Child.
