Anyone who's read any of my reviews before knows that I watch a crap-ton of hentai. Bearing this in mind, it's very hard for me to actually remember titles of hentai and for me to remember something, it has to be different. It has to be memorable or exceptionally good. Unfortunately, there are some hentai that are just so average and cliché that they are easily forgotten. This is one of them. I'm a girl, so sorry if my tastes are different to yours.

Ok, the story is basic. Except for the whole amnesia fandango, that's pretty new and exciting, right? I just feel that the amnesia prospect could have been done so much better, but oh well. They kind of introduce it, then throw it onto the back burner, and you forget that the girl was any different to how she currently is in the anime. In the beginning, you're introduced to a teenage couple. The girl is very loud and bubbly, and the guy is a bit quieter and... Amazingly bland too. Like, even for hentai standards, this guy is fucking boring as all hell. He's quite good looking, but that's all he has going for him. He's basically a douchebag who needs to learn how to not be a man-whore. Anyway, the girlfriend falls over and hits her head on the pavement, making herself forget a load of stuff (no pun intended), including who the main character guy is and she adopts a new personality, making her all moe and helpless, and shy about sex and all that. It's quite an interesting twist that I hadn't seen before, so I wish that they'd played it out a tad more to make the whole show more interesting. It revolves around this couple and the amnesia-chick's sister, who totally likes the bland dude as well, so she wants to get in on the action.

The art is ok, the character designs are quite fresh apart from the fact that the sister really looks like Haruhi Suzumiya, yellow hairband included. The animation is a bit clunky, but overall good and not too simplistic. It switches up positions and angles swiftly during the sex scenes, keeping it interesting. It also plays on some fetishes too. Sex in the locker room, girls in skimpy PE uniforms and some serious NTR. I guess it's reverse netorare though, since it's the guy who gets stolen from the amnesia chick by her sister. By the way, netorare is when a female character is stolen from her boyfriend by another character, and it revolves around the whole cheating side of things. This hentai goes pretty far though, to the point of him and the sister having risky sex in the same bed as the amnesia chick, who's trippin' on sleeping pills. It's pretty standard stuff, though. It's not much we haven't seen before.

The music isn't noticable at all, nor are the voice actors. Nobody really stood out for me, and the sex noises were actually quite boring in some parts.

Characters are very average too, just one guy trying to choose between sisters at the end of the day. The guy is just thinking with his junk, not his brain, and cheats on everyone like an asshole. He always has sex, then is like, "Oh shit! I shouldn't have done that!" It gets pretty tedious. The Haruhi sister is a slutty hoebag, who uses the amnesia to her advantage so she can steal the guy. The (now) quiet sister is quite cute, but annoyingly feeble and has no real personality. She's just a sex doll for the guy when he wants to be in charge.

Enjoyment! Obviously pretty good since it's a hentai. The story is terrible, but the sex scenes are actually good. They are animated quite nicely, and are all pretty fresh and cool. They're quite vanilla, except for sex in public places (the roof) and the whole NTR thing. Oh, and anal. I usually find anal stuff to be a bit cringey, but I found myself enjoying the scene in this one. It was nicely done! I enjoyed it quite a lot, except for the annoying story.

Overall, it's a standard modern hentai with just-above-average character designs, and decent animation. The sex scenes are actually very good, but it's the bland, cardboard cut-out characters that I didn't enjoy. The plot was going in a nice direction, and had a lot of potential, but when they realised they'd gone 5 minutes without some sex, they flipped the table and messed it all up. I'd definitely watch it for the sex, if you can stomach all of the facepalm-inducing characters. Spectacularly average. It's disappointing that the story and characters are so forgettable since some of the sex scenes in this were very good. Unfortunately, the annoying characters just grated on me, so it gets a 6 out of ten.
