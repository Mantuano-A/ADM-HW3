The plot and dialog lines are sometimes silly and easy excuses to make the hospitalized male character have sex with nurses, but I found this to be amusing.

The male character becomes mean to the nurses at some point, which made me feel less immersed in the anime. At least one of the girls is definitely turned into a slave.

Characters are drawn in a way that makes them look a little more realistic than your average anime. It might lean towards the uncanny valley at times but the girls usually look attractive.

Care is given to the shading and to the depth and coloring of background art to make it look quite realistic. I could almost recommend you watch this title just to have a look at these great images.

I have mixed feelings about the animation quality: there’s a large amount of frames but they somewhat don’t look as smooth as they should be when in motion, but overall it's fine.

Characters moan, talk and think a lot during sex. Whether you find it distracting or arousing is up to you.

The amount of censorship is heavy but many of the lengthy sex scenes have the characters move in a very sexy manner, and therefore censorship doesn’t impair enjoyment much.

There are a few shots here and there that look really nice and are actually memorable, like the first swallowing scene, one paizuri and one cowgirl position scene just to name a few.

Sex sound effects are quite noticeable and a little silly at times.

The highlight is the particularly lewd way most sex acts are shown in, especially the blowjob scenes.

Features nurses, swallowing, anal, paizuri, many cumshots, a bit of yuri and a gangbang (spit roast, brief DP).

I personally enjoyed this title quite well. Overall I’d say Chinetsu Karte features varied scenes, is above-average in quality and shouldn’t be missed by anyone who likes lewd sex acts, great blowjob scenes and the nurse theme.
