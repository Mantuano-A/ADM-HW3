I do not trust you if you don't want to own this car.

This. This is the best commercial to ever exist. It's a car designed for Char motherfucking Aznable. He uses it to drive to his robot when he needs to get to it fast.

This car actually exists.

You better believe that this is the closest we'll ever get to actual Gundams. This is the only technology from the Gundam universe to appear in our lives. Cherish it, my brothers and sisters. It's worthy of celebration.

Like, have you seen the photos of this beast? It's the most stupidly flashy car in existence. You know all those cringy photos of cars with anime stickers? You can actually get this one with anime stickers already on it! And not just any sticker, it's the Zeon sign, carefully painted onto the car.

You can literally buy a car with a sign of Gundam's interpretation of the Nazis. How is this real? The absolute mad lads in Japan allowed for this. Hail Zeon my dudes!

Also, there are apparently two colors. One's red for Char and one's green to look like the Zakus I guess. I just want to inform you, that if you ever even think about buying the green one instead of the red one, you're dead in my eyes.

So yeah, if it wasn't clear enough, I want this car, the red one. I accept any donations toward this goal. PM me if you want to help the cause.

This is a 40 seconds commercial featuring your favourite Gundam character: Char Aznable. The commercial opens with Char landing his MSN-04 Sazabi on a colony and walking towards the new Zeonic Toyota. After taking a good look at it one of the Zeon mechanics hands him the keys and Char drives.

What I liked:

+It was short but sweet
+Char Aznable returns
+No Amuro Ray!!
+Animation
+Music from first Gundam series
+Zeonic Toyota looks nice


Disliked:
-No real story
-Not enough Char
-No Gundam fights, there are no mobile suits other than the one Char used to land on the colony

Recommended if you want to see more of Char with 21st century art style and animation.

This advertisement does it's job. It gives a brief summary of the car, and uses a well known character (Char Aznable from the Gundam franchise) to advocate it.

Not much to say about a such a short commercial, except that the animation and art quality are quite nice.

The second commercial is about as good as the first, again involving Char and the new Toyota. The only thing new to this second commercial is a brief cameo from Lalah. Still, something about Char Aznable bragging about his car is way more entertaining than it should be...)

Would you buy a car if Char Aznable told you to?
