Gekkan Shoujo Nozaki Kun Specials is really just one episode split into 6 parts.
And since you've already seen the series, you know how awesome and funny this will be.
I'll give it to you as brief as possible.

This series of specials is about the beach event commonly found in romcom anime. Yeah, it's hilarious. The drama club takes a trip to the beach and nozaki and his team joins in. Some episodes are for the preparation of the trip and some show different situations at the beach. So, like I said before, this is an episode divided into 6 parts. You've got all your main characters in it and well, that's all it is. BEACH EVENT FTW!!!

Overall, if I see it as an episode of the series, I would rate is as one of the best.

For the main series of Gekkan Shoujo Nozaki-kun, I gave it a well-deserved 10. For the OVA, I'm afraid once again I need to grade what it deserves: a solid-but-not-that-impressive 8.

I believe this OVA requires some sort of a warning: if you want to enjoy it thoroughly, you'd better watch the whole six parts with no break in-between. Otherwise, you would feel as scattered as this OVA. This is basically a 24-minute episode broken down to six pieces but the execution feels so economical, one couldn't help but feel this OVA as more of an afterthought for the main series as opposed to being a long-awaited encore. If you want to think positively, perhaps the good people of Doga Kobo were simply holding their cards for the upcoming season (fingers crossed!). But if you don't, well, no need to worry because this OVA is still enjoyable-- it's just that we've seen the most joyous Gekkan Shoujo Nozaki-kun could whip up.

Ultimately, even for the best anime series out there, not all OVAs are winners and there's no obligation to being it in the first place. This OVA may even give you the craving to re-watch the entire series. No pressure, no expectation.

I'll put the TL;DR at the top: This is a great addition to the regular series, and I think it wraps up a couple of the subplots quite nicely. Even though the episodes are only three minutes long, its good for a little pick me up, or you can binge them all together. Like other reviews say, its basically one episode split into six parts, but I think that format works terrifically for these specials.

The specials take on the classic romance anime trope: the beach episode. Now if you've seen the regular series, you know that nothing will go the way you think it will! So if you've seen or are currently watching the series, you should definitely watch these shorts too! Its a great way to wrap up the story, kinda like a nice dessert after a fine meal. I wish I could grow a third hand so that I could give this entire series not two, but THREE thumbs up!
