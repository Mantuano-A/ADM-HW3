Never thought I'd see a anime disguised as a hentai, or is it the other way around?

I think this three episode series has some good bones to flesh out a pretty extraordinary full length show that would stick in people's minds for a real long time. Sure the student/teacher thing has been done to death, but this feels more mature and grounded. That, to me, is pretty refreshing. I actually wish to see more. I want to know where the two characters end up with each other without all the gags you would typically see in this type of formula.

Story, like I said is typical. It's the story's conveyance that hooked me. Like I said, it's done in a very mature way. I wish this was a more common occurrence.

The art and music is what you'd expect. It is really nice to look at though. The music is a notch above mediocrity and fits the tone nicely.

Overall, the characters could use some fleshing out, but this is perfectly fine for three episodes. Like I said, this is a solid foundation for a bigger and memorable show if something ever comes out of it.

I'm thoroughly pleased with the episodes overall except I wish there was either more 5 minute episodes or each episode was longer.
